NewsBriefs

Commonwealth Bank has partnered with Airtasker to allow customers to confirm their identity with a 'CommBank Identified' badge.
Teams of women from the Innovation Fellowship program will be invited into Government Departments as part of the $450,000 Code for Victoria II – Women in Tech Program. Women designers, programmers and user-experience experts will spend six months showcasing their skills while helping find new ways to improve government services.
Museums Victoria is going green through an innovative energy management partnership with Siemens and the Victorian Government. The initiative will improve the museum's sustainability efforts through the installation of new LED lighting, chillers and water systems. A co-generation unit will be added to enable the building to produce its own cleaner energy supplies.
The Queensland Government has commissioned Slingshot to run a Tourism, Travel, Entertainment and Leisure accelerator program. Slingshot will operate two cohorts simultaneously in Brisbane and Cairns.
Tenders for large scale solar plants comprising $150 million of new capital investment have been announced for regional Victoria. This initiative is anticipated to result in a reduction of more than 80,000 tonnes of greenhouse gas emissions every year, keeping Victoria on track to meet its target of net-zero emissions by 2050.
SoleNet and Sure Telecom have been banned from operating telco services for ' unconscionable conduct ', with James Harrison to pay $250,000 in penalties. He has been disqualified from managing corporations for three years.
Victorian paramedics will undertake new 360-degree virtual reality training to better protect themselves from violence when responding to volatile medical emergencies.
A new online lodgement portal will launch later this year by The Department of Planning and Environment in NSW. This portal will help builders and homeowners to prepare, submit, lodge and track development applications.
Superloop has established a new point of presence at NEXTDC 's C1 Canberra data centre.
A GPS koala tracking study in Wingecarribee has mapped more than 3000 koalas in the Southern Highlands, making it the largest known koala population in southern NSW.

Workplace rehabilitation provider Rehab Management has deployed Citrix 's SD-WAN technology.
Technology company Yojee (ASX: YOJ) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Subscribe to Food, which brings small batch food and wine products to Singapore.
The Australian Bureau of Statistics has confirmed the release and topic schedule for data from the 2016 Census. A preview of results will be released on Tuesday 11 April 2017.
The Fintel Alliance has been launched to combat terrorism financing and money laundering. The Alliance comprises 19 partners, including Australia's financial intelligence agency AUSTRAC, the Australian Federal Police, NSW Police, the ATO, the four big banks, Western Union and PayPal.
Infrastructure at the Australian Signals Directorate buildings in Canberra will receive a $75 million upgrade . The Australian Signals Directorate provides signals intelligence and information security to the Australian Government and the Australian Defence Force.
A new study from Universities Australia has shown that more than 4 in 5 startup founders in Australia are university graduates, and around 1 in 5 founders have benefited from an acceleration or incubation program.
The Department of Defence has partnered with CAE to provide comprehensive support and training for the Royal Australian Air Force’s Hawk MK127 training system. Valued at approximately $14.5 million over a 4.5 year period, the contract with CAE will provide Full Mission fighter simulators as well as maintenance and support services.
BAES Australia will promote Australian industry to the world through the online information portal , ‘Flight Path’, aiming to demonstrate the growing breadth of Australian defence industry capability.
Collaborate Corporation's (ASX: CL8) DriveMyCar business has partnered with ORIX Australia Corporation for ORIX vehicles to be rented through the DriveMyCar platform to private and corporate customers.

CompanyWatch

Telstra Ventures has invested in VeloCloud Networks, which has closed a US$35 million Series D round of funding . The round was led by Hermes Growth Partners, with other new investors including Khazanah Nasional Berhad, the strategic investment fund of the Government of Malaysia.
Artificial intelligence technology provider Kaplan has acquired Australian AI-based regtech startup, Red Marker.

PeopleWatch

Amanda Price is to head up KPMG Australia's new High Growth Ventures practice. Price was CEO of ad-tech startup AirWORKS Media, and VP of Business Development, Partner Relations and Operation for EYEcorp, and VP Innovation for Advance. She was also founder and CEO of ausEmerge.
After nine years, Phil Morle and the team at Pollenizer have decided to close their doors. An early mover in the innovation ecosystem, Pollenizer was one of the first to begin systematically supporting startups. While its directors continue to support their portfolio of startups, all consulting and education business will cease.
Local Measure has appointed Peter Hughes as VP Strategic Alliances. He was most recently at Cisco.
Tim Murphy is Country Manager for Arbor Networks . Murphy held previous positions at Southern Cross Protection, Tufin, RSA, Earthwave and Cisco.
