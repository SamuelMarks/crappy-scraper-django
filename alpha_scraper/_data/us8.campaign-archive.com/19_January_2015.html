news briefs

The Australian Bureau of Statistics and the Department of Communications have announced a joint project to review the nation’s ICT statistics. The project will identify opportunities to improve ICT statistics, to develop a framework for ongoing assessment of the digital economy, and improve the coordination and governance of information needs for the future.
The Federal Government is welcoming written submissions following the release of the draft legislation making improvements to the taxation arrangements for Employee Share Schemes. Amendments include the tax on the options is paid when there is an actual material value on the options and eligible start-up companies can issue shares or options to their employees at a small discount, generally exempt from up-front taxation.
Professor Graeme Clark AC from the University of Melbourne is the first Australian to receive the US Russ Prize for an outstanding achievement in bioengineering innovation that is in widespread use to improve health and well-being: the cochlear implant.
The School of Social Entrepreneurs Australia has committed to raising $1.5 million in the first five years of an initiative called Partnering for Scale and Impact aimed at transforming the social enterprise sector in Australia. SSE Australia was founded by Social Ventures Australia, SSE UK, and serial social entrepreneur Steve Lawrence AO.
The Top 20 Finalists in this year’s OzAPP Awards have been announced.
Moko Social Media (ASX: MKB) has partnered with David Koch’s Pinstripe Media to launch Tagroom Success to teach young entrepreneurs the nuts and bolts of business and finance. Tagroom Success will initially launch as a vertical within the current Tagroom site and will later develop into a stand-alone publication.
Westpac has informed OzForex Group (ASX:OFX) that it has made a strategic decision to exit the money service industry which means that Westpac will no longer be able to provide banking services to OzForex including - transactional banking, wholesale FX and deposits. OzForex has reassured its clients that it will leverage its existing financial institutional relationships to replace Westpac.

company watch

Trade Me (ASC:TME) has acquired a 15% stake in the New Zealand peer-to-peer lending platform Harmoney . The Auckland based company was founded in July 2013. The CFO of Trade Me, Jonathan Klouwens will join the Harmoney board.
Telstra Ventures has led a $14.5 million series D round of funding in US based company, Elemental , joined by Sky in the UK, along with participation from existing Elemental’s investors. Elemental provides software-defined video solutions for multiscreen content delivery.
iProperty Group Limited (ASX:IPP) has bought HongKong's Squarefoot from REA Group (ASX:REA) in a share deal increasing REA Group's shareholding in iProperty Group to 19.9%. REA's CFO, Owen Wilson, will join the iProperty Board.
Telstra (ASX: TLS) acquired Pacnet just before Christmas for US$697 million. The acquisition of the Singapore and Hong Kong headquartered Pacnet includes interests in China.
Fairfax Media Limited (ASX:FXJ) has acquired 100% of the issued shares in Metro Media Publishing Holdings (MMPH). Prior to this transaction Fairfax held 50% of MMPH with the remaining 50% held by the foundation MMPH shareholders. MMPH founder Antony Catalano will continue in his role as Chief Executive Officer of the Domain Group and the operations of MMPH will fall under his responsibility.
Tech23 alumni Bluedot Innovation has raised $2.5 million in funding led by US-based investor Jeffrey Katz. The company plans to use the funds to grow its US presence and growth capital.
Handkrafted has secured $100,000 in seed funding from Sydney Seed Fund led by Garry Visontay, Benjamin Chong, and Ari Klinger. The Sydney startup plans to use the fund for platform development and market expansion into new craft verticals and geographies.
Inabox Group (ASX:IAB) completed the acquisition of Anittel Group Limited on 1 January with majority shareholder support. The newly acquired business will continue to operate under the Anittel brand. Former Coca-Cola Amatil Senior Executive Vincent Pesquet has been appointed as Anittel’s CEO.

people watch

After spending the past 15 months setting up BuildingIQ in the US, Mike Zimmerman is returning to Australia with his family. Zimmerman founded BuildingIQ five years ago, after extracting IP from CSIRO . Although remaining a shareholder he is stepping away from BuildingIQ and is looking forward to making a difference in Australia’s exciting tech scene.
Kerry Purcell has been appointed Managing Director of IBM Australia and New Zealand. He was previously the Managing Partner of IBM Global Business Services in Japan.
The Information and Communications Technology industry in Geelong Victoria has appointed Aamir Qutub as its new General Manager. Qutub will be replacing Craig Hill.
MyriadIT has appointed Carl Wills as its new Client Services Manager.
Carla Glavonjic has joined Zuora as its first Marketing Manager for Australia and New Zealand. Glavonjic was previously ANZ Marketing Manager for LinkedIn.
Q Limited (ASX: QXQ) has announced it has completed its Share Purchase Agreement for the acquisition of Crowd Mobile and has appointed Theo Hnarakis, Domenic Carosa and Hans De Back as Directors. In addition, Domenic Carosa has been appointed Chief Executive Officer of Crowd Mobile.
