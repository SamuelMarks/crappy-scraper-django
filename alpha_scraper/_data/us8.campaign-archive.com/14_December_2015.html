news briefs

Congratulations to the TEAM at Atlassian (NASDAQ: TEAM) and especially the founders, Mike Cannon-Brookes and Scott Farquhar on a spectacular listing on NASDAQ Global Select Market . Atlassian is now valued at over $8 billion.
CSIRO welcomed news from the Federal Government’s National Innovation and Science Agenda with $70 million over 10 years committed to a CSIRO-managed innovation fund. This will be supported by an investment of new revenue from CSIRO's WLAN licensing and CSIRO will seek private sector investment to form a fund of approximately $200 million. As part of the package, $5 million per year is committed to expand CSIRO's accelerator program to include other publically funded research organisations. In addition to this CSIRO has received $25 million per year increased funding dedicated to its data science efforts through Data61 .
AARNet , Australia’s Academic and Research Network has launched the Australia Wide-Area Software Defined Networking (SDN) Testbed with a consortium of R&D partners. The consortium of researchers from CSIRO Data61 and nine universities, led by the University of New South Wales , secured LIEF (Linkage Infrastructure, Equipment and Facilities) grant funding from the Australian Research Council to deploy SDN equipment within each of their labs.
Telstra (ASX: TLS) has announced an in-principle commitment of $10 million plus in-kind support over the next five years to University of New South Wales (UNSW)-based Australian Research Council Centre for Quantum Computation and Communication Technology on the same day that the Commonwealth Bank of Australia also pledged another $10 million, on top of its $5 million investment in December 2014. (FYI, Physics World , the magazine of the UK’s Institute of Physics, has named the advance in quantum computing involving engineers at UNSW among its global “Top Ten Breakthroughs of 2015”.)
La Trobe University has partnered with SAS Australia to offer a Master of Business Analytics degree, with the first student intake scheduled for 2016.
Tech23 2015 Alumni and University of Sydney Incubate graduate, Abyss Solutions , is one of the ten startups to be accepted into the third intake for muru-D kicking off in February 2016. Other companies include Drive Yello , Helian Systems , Persolla, Quberider and Simpla .
Australia Post has partnered with Chinese B2B online wholesale website 1688.com which was established in 2010 by the Alibaba Group (NYSE: BABA). Initially they will sell Australian wine but plan to offer new categories in 2016.
The Tasmanian Government has funded six contracts valued at $105 million. The successful tenderers are TasmaNet , Tasmanian Networks , Telstra , and TPG with a total contract value of about $95 million over the next 13 years. New data centre services will initially be provided by TasmaNet and Tasmanian Networks in an estimated spend of $10.4 million over the next 13 years.
Mobile Embrace (ASX: MBE) has entered into an agreement with Asian telecommunications company Axiata , to provide direct carrier billing for its 260 million mobile subscribers.
CSIRO has entered into an agreement with Melbourne-based fine chemical manufacturer Boron Molecular , which is a CSIRO spin-out, to license CSIRO technologies with CSIRO receiving royalty on all sales. Technologies that fall under the agreement are wide-ranging, including polymers used in the biomedical industry and specialist fine chemicals for polymer manufacture.
Communications Minister Mitch Fifield is calling on nominations of mobile black spot locations around Australia to be submitted by Thursday 31 December 2015 for consideration under Round 2 of the Mobile Black Spot Programme.
The Federal Court has ruled against deducting payments from teachers' salaries for their work laptops resulting in some 46,000 Victorian teachers sharing in about $37 million in repayments.
CSR's Bradford division is to sell and install Powerwall , the Tesla battery system for residential and light commercial applications that enables the storage of energy such as solar power.

company watch

Uniseed , a venture fund operating at the Universities of Melbourne , New South Wales and Queensland has welcomed the addition of University of Sydney and CSIRO to launch Uniseed’s third and largest investment fund to date - $50 million investment fund to help commercialise research in Australia.
Blue Sky Venture Capital , a division of Blue Sky Alternative Investments (ASX: BLA) has announced it will launch a new $200 million venture capital fund targeted at institutional investors to invest in later-stage deals in Australia and overseas.
Shoes of Prey has closed US$15.5 million Series B round led by BlueSky Venture Capital with Greycroft and US retailer Nordstrom coming on board as equity partners. The round brings the total capital raised by the company since it was founded in 2009 to US$24.6 million.
Local shopping app Booodl has raised $2.85 million in a strategic investment round led by Scentre Group (ASX: SCG), the owner and operator of Westfield across Australia and New Zealand.
Perth based location analytics provider, Inhouse Group has raised $900,000 in a strategic funding round led by Peter Rossdeutscher, who has also joined Inhouse as the Board Chairman. Other key shareholders taking part in the funding round include Zhenya Tsvetnenko, Sir Robin Miller, the Chair and Deputy Chair of the WA Angel Investors and CSA Global .
iSelect Limited (ASX: ISU) has ceased negotiations with Providence Equity Partners due to recent volatility in market and management which would reduce the result of securing a reasonable deal for shareholders.
First State Super has partnered with H2 Ventures to invest in Australian fintech start-ups. First State Super anticipates investing around $250 million.
