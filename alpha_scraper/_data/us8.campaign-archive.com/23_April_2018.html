NEWS BRIEFS
The Global Startup Ecosystem Report 2018 survey has recognised Melbourne and Sydney as “health and adtech ecosystems to watch”. Sydney was also named the #1 location in the Asia Pacific, and one of the world’s top ten, for “local connectedness”.
Unlockd is delaying its IPO as it seeks an injunction in the UK High Court to prevent Google from disabling AdMob generated advertising content and removing Unlockd apps from the Google Play Store. Unlockd’s legal counsel has confidence that the threats made by Google represent an abuse of its dominant position and a breach of competition rules.
The Federal Government has released a report on the findings of the review of the .au Domain Administration (auDA).The review’s key finding is that the current management framework is no longer fit-for-purpose .
More accurate GPS technology on specially fitted out aeroplanes is being trialled under the Satellite-Based Augmentation System (SBAS) for the Australasian region - one of 28 projects from across 10 industry sectors being conducted as part of the trial. The Australasian trial of SBAS is being led by Geoscience Australia in partnership with Land Information New Zealand and, GMV, Inmarsat and Lockheed Martin and funded by the NZ and Australian Governments. The Cooperative Research Centre for Spatial Information is managing all 28 industry projects.
A partnership enabling collaboration between Indian industry and the NSW startup, tech and advanced manufacturing sectors is now in play. Worth $1.58 million over two years, the partnership will enable NSW startups to travel to India and foster collaboration and commercial connections.
Researchers at The University of Queensland have developed new nanotechnology to monitor the diversity of individual cancer cells circulating in the body in partnership with the Olivia Newton John Cancer Research Institute.
The Workplace Safety Futures study from CSIRO’s Data61 in partnership with Safe Work Australia has revealed six ‘megatrends’ shaping the future workforce environment. The trends are: the extending reach of automated systems and robotics, rising workplace stress and mental health issues, rising screen time, sedentary behaviour and chronic illness, blurred boundaries between work and home, the gig and entrepreneurial economy, and an ageing workforce.
Image resources NL (ASX: IMA) has announced the execution of a binding Heads of Agreement with Sunrise Energy Group for the development of a 3-4 Megawatt Alternating Current Solar Farm to be built next to the company’s Boonanarring mineral sands.
Adacel Technologies Limited (ASX: ADA) has signed a multi-phase contract with Navegacao Aerea de Portugal to upgrade its existing air traffic management system. The system is used by Nat Portugal for oceanic, en route, approach and tower control within the Santa Maria Flight Information region.
A $7 million Innovation Precinct has been officially opened at Swinburne University ’s Hawthorn campus in Victoria.
The NT government has committed $66.9 million to develop an integrated client IT system and data brokerage service for child protection and youth justice .
The Sydney Environment Institute and the City of Sydney, along with partners at UNSW, TAFE NSW and FoodLab Detroit in the U.S., have committed to collaborate on a pilot project, Sydney Food Business Incubator.
The Education Council’s Optimising STEM Industry-School Partnerships report has been released . The report’s chair Dr Alan Finkel AO confirmed his “firm belief that industry-school partnerships have important, even life-changing impacts.”
COMPANY WATCH
Equinix (Nasdaq: EQIX) has completed its $1.04 billion acquisition of Australian rival Metronode from the Ontario Teachers Pension Plan.
WiseTech Global has acquired three logistics solutions providers: Latin American based Forward, Softcargo and French based Easylog.
Telstra Ventures has taken a stake in web-based DevOps platform company GitLab in a move it says will allow it to leverage its application to support innovation and optimise collaborative workflows.
Solar energy retailing company DC Power Co. has raised $2.5 million from over 15,000 investors in an equity crowdfunding campaign.
Telehealth startup Coviu , which spun out of the CSIRO’s Data61 arm, has raised $1 million from Main Sequence Ventures.
Queensland startup BenchOn has raised $650,000 in seed funding.
Employment platform DCC Jobs has rebranded to WORK180 and has raised $1 million in seed funding, as it continues on its mission to connect women with roles at organisations with a focus on gender equality. The seed round was led by Skip Capital, (co-CEO of Atlassian, Scott Farquhar’s private investment fund), with other investors including the co-founders of Lux Group, Thomas Rice, Lead Tech Analyst at Perpetual, and Startmate.
Medtech startup Global Kinetics received $7.75 million from the Federal Government’s Biomedical Translation Fund. The company has also received funding from the Michael J. Fox Foundation, the Shake It Up Australia Foundation and Parkinson’s Victoria.
Online shopping rewards platform Cashrewards has received its first external funding, closing a $5.25 million investment from Silicon Valley fund Partners For Growth.
Sydney-based investment house Macdoch Group has taken a lead investor role in European Internet of Things energy company GreenCom Networks. GreenCom is looking to enter the Australian market.
