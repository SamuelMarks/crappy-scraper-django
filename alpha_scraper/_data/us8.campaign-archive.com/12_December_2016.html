news briefs

CSIRO’s ON Accelerate3 has selected 10 public funded research teams who will commence the 12 week program in mid January to develop business planning, commercialisation and pitching skills.
University of Western Australia researchers are modifying bushfire simulation software Australis into a high-end tablet to provide accurate predictions of fire behaviour more rapidly than current methods.
Telstra Ventures has published Strategic Growth Investing: The Next Evolution of Corporate Venture Capital . The paper's co-author Mark Sherman (MD, Telstra Ventures) commented that VC is a major driving force behind technology-led disruption.
Australia's Chief Scientist Dr Alan Finkel has presented the Preliminary Report of the National Electricity Market Security Review. Finkel commented : "We now have a once-in-a-generation opportunity to reform the electricity sector".
The inaugural Cicada Innovations Nobel Gala celebrated advanced technology in a memorable event attended by industry partners and stakeholders, highlighting companies Nano-X , Trimph , Marathon Targets , and Law of the Jungle . Collaboration awards were presented to ezyCollect , PrimeX Connect, Trimph and Upguard. Bill Ferris, the Chairman of Innovation Australia, congratulated Cicada CEO Petra Andren and emphasised the importance of sharing stories to encapsulate the impact of advanced technology for Australia.
AgileCraft is opening its Asia Pacific headquarters in Melbourne early in 2017, with plans to create up to 60 new jobs. AgileCraft is also to be a gold sponsor of Agile Australia 2017 .
Suretek and Optus have announced a three-year, $40 million contract extension for the delivery of Suretek's 1345 Securecall services.
A free educational app commissioned by the Australian Government and developed by the ABC allows users to explore the First World War Battles of Fromelles and Pozières. The app features a 3D map and contemporary diary entries narrated by Hugo Weaving. It is the second in a proposed series of six apps that will be produced during the Anzac Centenary period, 2014-2018.
Australian startup PADLOKT has announced partnerships with the UFC and several athletes from the NBA, NFL and cricket.
LiveHire (ASX: LVH) is expanding into healthcare , signing and launching Talent Communities for aged care providers Japara Healthcare (ASX: JHC) and Amana Living Inc.
The Australian Government will pilot a new classification tool to streamline the process of classifying Netflix content. The tool combines Netflix's technology with Australia's classification ratings and consumer advisories. The pilot will take place over the next 12 months, during which time Netflix will use the tool to classify their content for Australian audiences.
Vault Intelligence Limited (ASX: VLT) is partnering with China-based Beckwell EHS Consulting to provide its enterprise and mobility solutions.
Powerco has selected Broadspectrum in New Zealand to design and build a mobile radio network across their North Island electricity distribution area. Hytera will provide the DMR Tier III trunked system hardware for the contract.
3D printing technology could help those suffering from sleep apnoea through a partnership between Oventus and CSIRO . Anti-snoring devices customised to individual needs will be created at the new Oventus 3D printing facility, which has opened at CSIRO's Clayton site.
Tribal Group has announced a $1.17 million investment to establish its Asia Pacific headquarters in Geelong, which will boost the region’s tech sector and create 50 new jobs.
Cyber security specialists came together last week for the 360° Cyber Game , which was held at ANU in partnership with RAND Corporation .
The Productivity Commission is recommending that the current Telecommunications Universal Service Obligation be replaced by "a more targeted approach" which recognises the public investment in the NBN. Written submissions can be made until 20 January 2017.
Tuned Global is partnering with Japanese streaming service LINE MUSIC to expand its music catalogue.
A new $662 million wind farm near Horsham in Victoria’s west has been given the green light. The wind farm operators RES are providing a community fund to the value of $1000 per turbine per year to support local projects and causes.
In other clean energy news, Zenviron and Vestas have been awarded a $430 million contract for the delivery of the 270MW Sapphire Wind Farm in northern NSW.
The Victorian Government is considering a market-led proposal from Nokia and Vodafone to upgrade Melbourne's train system network to 4G technology, support extra services, provide more timely and detailed maintenance alerts, as well as more accurate arrival times, and high-capacity signalling.
A new state wide drone program will be used by Environment Protection Authority Victoria to capture video evidence of illegal dumping.
The City Solutions Platform will support the City of Sydney and advise on the best way to invest $10 million for accelerating renewable energy.
Brisbane firms Axalon, Liquid State and Vuid, and Redcliffe joint venture MacroGIS and FlowBiz are collaborating with the Queensland Government to showcase their solutions to identified ICT challenges in the first round of an innovative procurement program for Queensland ICT providers. The 12-week Testing Within Government program gave each company $25,000 in Advance Queensland support to develop and test their products in collaboration with department personnel.
The first new fibre is being rolled out in South Australia to connect key innovation precincts to the $4.7 million GigCity Adelaide ultra-fast internet network .
South Australian students interested in computer programming will join millions of others around the world this week in the Hour of Code , an annual event held during Computer Science Education Week.
Complexica will deploy its order management system under a new contract with UK-based Bunzl .
Western Australian Police are now equipped with a 3D camera that uses laser technology to capture and replicate precise vehicle crash scenes, allowing roads to reopen quickly and reducing congestion.
$11.8 million in grants is now available to Western Australian grower groups and agribusinesses to stimulate research, innovation and collaboration along the supply chain.
Samsung and the University of Canberra will work together to develop a technology-driven learning centre to improve science and mathematical literacy among young Australians.

company watch

Spirit Telecom Limited (ASX: ST1) will acquire Phone Names Marketing Pty Ltd and its associated entity, Phone Names, for $4.2 million. The acquisition will be funded through an equity capital raising of $2.25 million and a proposed new debt facility.
Dubber Corporation Limited (ASX: DUB) has completed a $6.345 million capital raising . The funds will support the company's expansion in North America, establish a team of on-site tier-one service provider account executives, and help to accelerate the uptake of Dubber Playback. After the placement's completion, Thorney Investment Group will become a substantial shareholder.
Over the Wire (ASX: OTW) has entered into a binding agreement to acquire ANZ telecommunications solutions provider Telarus by early January 2017. Over The Wire hopes the acquisition will help its geographic expansion into Victoria.
Nearmap Limited's (ASX: NEA) share purchase plan has opened, with a maximum amount of $5 million to be raised.
The ACCC will not oppose the proposed acquisition of Australian Regional Media by News Corporation (ASX: NSW) from APN News and Media (ASX: APN).

people watch

Simon Corbell has been appointed to the recently established position of Victorian Renewable Energy Advocate (VREA).
Professor Ian O’Hara is to be Queensland’s biofutures ambassador, spearheading the growth industry’s drive into the Asia Pacific.
Datto is expanding into ANZ, with senior hires including Simran Kaur, Marketing Manager APAC and James Hatt, Channel Sales Executive, both of whom will support James Bergl, recently promoted to Regional Director ANZ, and Lauren Escalona, promoted to Channel Sales Executive.
Scott Wilson has been appointed to the Board of iSelect Limited (ASX: ISU) as Managing Director and CEO, effective from 3 January 2017. Wilson was most recently appointed Chair of the iMoney Group, Malaysia.
