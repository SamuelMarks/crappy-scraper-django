NewsBriefs

StartupAus has released Crossroads 2017 , a report with an action plan for the growth and maturation of the Australian startup ecosystem. Scott Farquhar, co-founder of Atlassian, writes a compelling introduction to this year’s report, calling for immediate action on visas to help Australian startups access the world’s best talent.
Congratulations to Quantum physicist Professor Michelle Yvonne Simmons from the University of NSW who has been awarded 2018’s Australian of the Year for her work in the "space race of the computing era". Mathematics teacher Eddie Woo was awarded 2018’s Australia’s Local Hero , for helping kids all around the country learn and understand the subject through video tutorials.
CSIRO is to partner with San Francisco-based ocean technology startup Saildrone, to improve measurement and monitoring in Australian waters and the Southern Ocean. Australian Saildrone founder and CEO Richard Jenkins said CSIRO provided a unique opportunity for marine research collaboration in the Southern Hemisphere.
Melbourne-based software company Promapp solutions is partnering with the NZ programme GoShift in order to supply a Quality Management System .
A civilian aerial surveillance service has begun across the Central and Western Pacific region as part of the Australian Government’s $2 billion Pacific Maritime Security Program.
Proposals are being received from industry, academia and government research agencies to contribute to the development of quantum technologies for Defence. Applications close on 13 February 2018.
CSIRO and Swinburne have launched a new partnership in Silicon Valley as part of a strategic move to increase Australia-US research, innovation and commercialisation. Swinburne joins medical device company Anatomics as one of the first Australian companies to co-locate with CSIRO.
Boeing and CSIRO will perform new joint research and development in space technologies. Scientists from the US and Australia will collaborate on areas which are focused on the developing needs of the Australian space market.
Australia’s .au Domain Administration (auDA) is calling on industry and key stakeholders to pitch in on proposed changes to the management of the country’s top level domain. With a total of 16 questions posed in the newly released Issues Paper, auDA is seeking feedback on the current .au structure, reserved names, eligibility and allocation rules, and domain name licence conditions.

CompanyWatch

Main Sequence Ventures is backing Indeelabs , who are developing gene-modify cell therapy.
Sports social platform 20FOUR (ASX:CHP) is planning to enter the ASX in a reverse takeover later this year.
Sydney-based airport transport startup Jayride (ASX:JAY) has listed on the ASX after an oversubscribed $1.5 million initial public offering (IPO).
Queensland agriculture supply chain tracking startup BlockGrain has raised $1 million, or 800,000 worth of XEM tokens, through the global NEM blockchain investment fund.
British-based private equity firm Permira , is to pay $1.3 billion to acquire Australia's biggest diagnostic imaging provider I-Med .
Tech startup GetSwift’s (ASX:GSW) shares remain suspended by the ASX this week, pending further enquiries from the ASX about how the details of GetSwift’s partnerships were announced to investors.

PeopleWatch

FinTech Australia has appointed Stuart Stoyan as chairman. Stoyan is currently CEO at MoneyPlace.
Robbie Cooke has been named the new CEO for Tyro . Cooke’s past roles include heading up Tatts Group and Wotif.
Annie Parker , former interim CEO at Fishburners, is to join Microsoft as global head of startups.
Cloud security company FirstWave (ASX:FCT) has appointed Dr Jai Menon as Strategic Advisor to the Chief Operating Officer. Menon is currently Group CTO of HT Media (India). FirstWave also announced the appointment of Shekila Ramalingam as Vice President, Business Development APAC.
