news briefs

Dr Alan Pearce and Braden Wilson have created brainBAND , a wearable technology designed to assist research into concussion in sports. The prototype was developed through Samsung Electronic Australia's Launching People program, which brings together two experts from different backgrounds to solve challenges facing society.
Protean Wave Energy Ltd (ASX: POW) plans to commence deployment of up to 30 Protean wave energy converter buoys at the Port of Bunbury, under the terms of a non-exclusive license agreement with the Southern Ports Authority. The Demonstration Wave Farm will be deployed from April 2016 for a trial period of up to six months.
NAB has announced a strategic alliance with the University of Melbourne and Melbourne Business School to support innovation and collaboration between the business and university sector. The agreement will 'facilitate the exchange of talent and ideas between NAB Labs and the University, to support joint design and research projects.' The alliance will include 'innovation practicums' in NAB Labs offered to students at Melbourne Business School, as well as custom education programs for NAB executives, applied research projects and NAB Labs' participation in the innovation precincts around the University.
SwarmFarm has launched round-the-clock robotic sprayers which use targeted infra-red beams to spray herbicides. The lightweight robots can 'talk' to each other and work cooperatively in a swarm. SwarmFarm was founded by farmers Andrew and Jocie Bate who partnered with Queensland University of Technology, University of Sydneyâ€™s Centre for Field Robotics and attracted backing from a number of sponsors.
The Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC) has authorised (with some conditions) the joint venture arrangement involving Cabcharge (ASX:CAB), Silver Top, Yellow and Black and White Cabs to launch and operate their new smartphone taxi booking app, ihail .
An international team of researchers based at the University of Sydney has generated single photons - the single quanta of light particles - as carriers of quantum information in security systems. According to the research team, the findings could 'revolutionise cyber security, along with advancing quantum computing'.
Eyre Peninsula's Cowell Electric Pty Ltd has been awarded a $20.4 million, five year Remote Areas Energy Supplies contract to supply energy services to 25 off-grid South Australian communities, including 15 Aboriginal communities.
The first stage of an online Digital Marketplace led by the Digital Transformation Office will help smaller businesses to apply for government ICT work. A series of targeted panels of providers will be established, the first of which is a digital specialists' panel. Discovery on the Digital Marketplace begins in April 2016, with a public Beta expected by the end of the year.
The Government requests feedback on ways to improve the delivery of the National Relay Service (NRS) through a recently published consultation paper Communications Accessibility: 2016 and Beyond .
A consortium of Australian motoring clubs (Queensland, West Australia, South Australia) has chosen Hexagon Safety & Infrastructure to provide a cloud-based platform for roadside assistance.
The Federal Government has released a ' response to Australia's FinTech priorities '. The statement, ' Backing Australian FinTech ', is available here .
OnMarket BookBuilds has partnered with BMY Group to supply content to the news and finance organisation's target market of Chinese-speaking investors.
Anti counterfeit technology company YPB Group Limited (ASX: YPB) has received an order for 700,000 vital record documents for the State of Guanajuato in Mexico. This is the second order resulting from a six-year joint venture with Affyrmx LLC.
NEXTDC Limited 's (ASX: NXT) C1 Canberra data centre has been connected to the AXONVX virtual exchange.
Smartpay (ASX: SMP) has launched its D-Series MPOS integrated mobile payment terminals and its Smartlink integrated payments software.
KNeoMedia Limited (ASX: KNM) is developing a multi-language range of KNeoWorld Apps for smartphones and tablets. The company is also launching Spanish and English language versions of KNeoWorld in the US market, and working with Chinese distribution partner SmartTrans (ASX: SMA) to test and develop a Mandarin version of the app.
Knosys Ltd (ASX: KNO) in conjunction with reseller partner Corporate Innovations (S) Pte. Ltd has signed a memorandum of understanding with the Singapore Asia Pacific operations of CITIC Telecom International (SEA) Pte Ltd for the deployment of the Knosys Platform.
Dubber Corporation Limited (ASX: DUB) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with MyNetFone, a subsidiary of MNF Group (ASX: MNF) to deliver a cloud based call recording solution on a large scale for users of traditional landlines.

company watch

carsales.com Limited (ASX: CAR) has acquired an 83% controlling shareholding in Chileautos for US$15 million, and retains an option to purchase the remaining 17% stake over the next four years.
OneVentures has closed a $19.7 million (US$15 million) financing round for Clinical Genomics Ltd , to launch its proprietary next-generation blood-based diagnostic tool for colorectal cancer (CRC). The funding will be used to advance commercialisation of the two-gene blood test, a 'Liquid Biopsy' for post-surgical monitoring of CRC recurrence, developed with the CSIRO. Clinical Genomics will launch its new test in collaboration with partner and shareholder Quest Diagnostics. OneVentures Partner and Managing Director Dr Paul Kelly will join Clinical Genomics' board of directors.
Alcidion Group Limited (ASX:ALC) listed on the ASX with a $33 million market capitalisation and $7 million in cash. The company has also secured an Implementation Planning Study for a large hospital which is part of an Australian Private Hospital group. Following the study, the Private Hospital Group is expected to pilot the technology across a 650-bed acute care facility.
Dematic has acquired Australian-based provider of Automated Guided Vehicles and software, NDC Automation .
Slingshot has acquired Melbourne-based accelerator program AngelCube which will be headed by Tom LeGrice. AngelCube founders Nathan Sampimon and Adrian Stone will assist Slingshot with strategic direction and be mentors for the new entity which plans to commit $10 million towards Victorian based startups.
Medical Director (formerly Health Communication Network or HCN) has been sold by Primary Health Care Limited for $155 million to Affinity Equity Partners.
