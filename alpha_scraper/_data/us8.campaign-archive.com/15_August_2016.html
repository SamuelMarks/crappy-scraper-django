news briefs

Following last week's Census debacle , the Government Cyber Security Adviser Alastair MacGibbon will head what Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull called "a very thorough review of the events", supported by the Australian Signals Directorate, the Treasury and the ABS.
A $1.5 million robotic gym has opened at the South Australian Women's and Children's Hospital. Two robotic machines, the Lokomat and Armeo, have been installed, as well as the Dynavision, a tool to assist visual awareness and eye-hand coordination.
NetComm Wireless Limited (ASX: NTC) has established an R&D facility in Sunrise, Florida. It is the company's first R&D facility outside of Australia. NetComm Wireless is also set to expand its Sydney and Melbourne-based facilities.
Telstra will invest up to $3 billion over three years on 'next generation network leadership, digitisation and customer experiences'. Details of the investment program will be confirmed progressively over the next two years, and will include 'short term actions to remove customer pain points' as well as longer-term improvements, including transforming the next generation of networks and providing rural and regional mobile technology.
AGL will begin a three year program next year to 'transform the customer's digital experience' with AGL. This will include investments in core technologies, processes and people. AGL plan to embed Lean and Agile processes throughout its organisation.
Senator Mitch Fifield, Minister for Communications, has used Netflix 's latest ISP Speed Index to support the Government's nbn rollout.
Tech23 Alumni social platform Local Measure has integrated with Cisco Spark.
CMC Markets has partnered with OnMarkets BookBuilds to enable CMC Markets' clients to bid for IPOs through their broking account using OnMarket's platform.
A new training program will give students and trainers time on board the Marine National Facility research vessel Investigator . The CAPSTAN (Collaborative Australian Postgraduate Sea Training Alliance Network) program provides practical training on a multi-disciplinary ocean class research vessel.
Think Tank 'Beyond Zero Emissions' has launched a report which explores how Australians can reduce greenhouse gas emissions within 10 years by using electric vehicles .
Technology developed in Queensland is helping to ease traffic congestion in America. Managed Motorways technology, developed by Transmax , will be trialled on Colorado highways.
Sydney fintech startup Fincast has announced a partnership with a Singaporean bank.
Recent field trials of Sigma Space's Mini Micro-Pulse LiDAR remote sensing technology indicate the feasibility of integrating this method with EnviroSuite.

company watch

Biopharmaceutical company Protagonist Therapeutics - founded at the University of Queensland - listed on the NASDAQ last week with a US$90 million IPO . Uniquest and Starfish Ventures are both shareholders.
BPS Technology Limited (ASX: BPS) has completed its underwritten $27.5 million institutional placement. Proceeds of the capital raising will be used to acquire Entertainment Publications Australia and New Zealand.
Sendle has raised $5 million in a Series A funding round. Full Circle Venture Capital led the funding round, along with a number of other VC firms including Black Sheep Capital, rampersand and Giant Leap, with NRMA as a returning investor.
Freelancer Limited (ASX: FLN) has acquired online escrow service Escrow Angel and its automotive brand Protecti.
Telstra will return up to $1.5 billion of capital to shareholders, comprising a $1.25 billion off-market buy-back and $250 million on-market share buy-back.

people watch

VoiceBox have announced Mark Johnson as their Chief Scientific Officer for VoiceBox Technologies Australia. Johnson holds a professorship at Macquarie University, and will lead innovation for Voice Artificial Intelligence.
Former Accenture regional managing director Jane Hemstritch is joining the Telstra Board as a non-executive Director.
Blueprint Group has appointed Zenardia Anderberg as General Manager - Group Business. Anderberg was previously at Crossmark Asia Pacific.
Karen Lawson has been named CEO of Slingshot - she was previously Head of Innovation.
Citrix 's new Group Vice President of Sales and Services for the Asia Pacific and Japan Region is Stanimira Koleva. She was previously at Software AG.
Check Point 's new Channel Sales Director Australia is Craig McGregor - previously of Symantec.
