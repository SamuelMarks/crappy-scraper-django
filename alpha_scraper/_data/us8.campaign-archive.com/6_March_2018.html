NewsBriefs

CSIRO has unveiled a fleet of Saildrones - solar and wind powered autonomous vehicles - which will leave Hobart to measure and monitor sea-surface temperature, salinity, ocean carbon, and biota - helping to paint a more detailed picture of the ocean in the Gippsland Basin. The Saildrones have been acquired through funding provided by the CO2CRC and the Australian Government's Education Investment Fund Support for Clean Energy Research Infrastructure.
Telstra (ASX: TLS) has announced a strategic partnership with the National Farmers Federation to grow Australia’s agricultural industry.
The Australian Government has signed a new UK-Australia Fintech Bridge to deepen ties between Australian and UK governments and regulatory agencies to accelerate both countries’ positions as leading global FinTech centres.
Tasmanian Government has selected MNF Enterprise as the preferred supplier for telephony and IP-based services.
The latest round of the Entrepreneurs’ Programme grant recipients has been released . The 14 innovations receiving funding included $669k to Inquisitive for their online artificial intelligent assistant for School Teachers; Tech23 Alumni Liquid Instruments received $774k; Personify Care received $409k and Hammer Technologies received $683k to launch their safety system in the US.
Australian crypto blockchain company Power Ledger is consulting with the Australian Taxation Office to develop a proposition on how initial coin offerings should be treated in Australia.
Congratulations to Cicada Innovations who was awarded the IMPACT Award - Technology Category by the International Business Innovation Association, which recognises Cicada Innovations as the home of deep tech.
Unisys has linked with Australia’s Department of Home Affairs to provide new biometric identification system to facilitate border identity checking and improve the ability to identify criminals and persons of national security interest.
Telstra (ASX: TLS) and Microsoft have jointly launched cloud voice services in Australia with the delivery of Telstra Calling for Office 365.
Three Australian AgTech companies - The Yield, AgriDigital and SmartShepherd - won awards at The World AgriTech Innovation Summit in San Francisco this week.
LaunchVic has invested $2.9 million to providers to offer training to Victorian startup founders .
Two large-scale, grid-connected batteries in Victoria are to receive$25 million in funding by the Federal Government through the Australian Renewable Energy Agency and matched by $25 million from the Victorian Government . The batteries will be located near Kerang and in Ballarat, and will collectively provide 55MW of energy and 80MWh of storage capacity.
CSIRO has launched the Sunrise Industries Report , which identifies the top seven industries within the ASEAN and neighbouring nations to inform decision makers on potential areas of regional growth, international collaboration and job creation.

CompanyWatch

Singaporean data-driven VC fund Hatcher+, is partnering with BlueChilli to co-invest in and build 240 global startups . The investment will be made from Hatcher+’s second fund worth US$125 million, and the deals will be identified by BlueChilli. For BlueChilli startups, Hatcher+ will provide US$200,000 each as follow-on funding when they raise capital upon completion of the programme.
The Boardroom has secured a seven figure investment for a minority stake in Western Australia-based employee management startup Definitiv .
Biteable, has received $2.8 million in funding from Tank Stream Ventures and Microequities Venture Capital Fund. Biteable which enables anyone to make a video, was the first Tasmanian company to ever present at Tech23 !
Trimantium GrowthOps (ASX:TGO) has debuted on the ASX following an IPO which raised $70 million. Through the IPO, 15 entrepreneurs have merged their eight specialist businesses to create GrowthOps - including 3wks, jtribe, Digital Moshi, KDIS, AJF Partnership, Khemistry, Voodoo Creative, and the Institute of Executive Coaching and Leadership (IECL).
Sydney University startup Somnium Labs has officially launched its crowdfunding campaign for the MuTu Pillow , which uses air-suspension technology.
Sydney-based provider of virtual data rooms, Ansarada has raised $24 million in an Ellerston-led round and is still on track for an IPO in two years.
Pacific Energy will acquire Contract Power at its $90 million enterprise value. The acquisition will be debt funded and will complete a $20 million rights issue.
Human resources technology startup Employment Hero has closed an $8 million in funding round from Seek and OneVentures .
Probe Group , in partnership with funds advised by Five V Capital, has entered into an agreement to acquire Salmat’s Contact business.
