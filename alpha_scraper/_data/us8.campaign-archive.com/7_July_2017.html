NewsBriefs

The New South Wales Government will invest $35 million into developing a new startup and innovation hub, the Sydney Startup Hub . Tenants will include Stone and Chalk, Fishburners, Tank Stream Labs and The Studio.
Harmful online gambling behaviour will be investigated through a new $300,000 research project funded by the Commonwealth Government ($200,000) and the Victorian Responsible Gaming Foundation ($100,000). Central Queensland University has been commissioned to analyse wagering industry data to develop a list of behavioural indicators and a predictive algorithm that can detect people displaying harmful betting patterns.
The South Australian Government is funding new software that aims to reduce the likelihood of workplace injuries and deaths on farms. The software developer, Safe Ag Systems will receive funding of up to $498,000 through the South Australian Early Commercialisation Fund.
Northrop Grumman Australia has been awarded a $223 million contract by the Australian Defence Force to deliver a new satellite ground station at Wagga Wagga, NSW. Northrop Grumman Australia will work with its partners ViaSat and Optus to deliver this new facility.
WaterGroup has signed a five-year partnership with Thinxtra to enable the capturing and transmission of data on water use via the Sigfox wireless network.
The first of nine electric trucks will soon be unveiled in Victoria. The manufacturer SEA Automotive received funding of up to $517,000 from the Victorian State Government last year to develop commercial electric vehicles. The trucks features a power pack system that can be used to power new medium duty vehicles or repower existing diesel trucks.
VR technology is being used to help treat people’s fears and phobias . Sydney-based Totem Labs received an MVP grant from Jobs for NSW to develop the technology for the Sydney Phobia Clinic.
Culture Amp has declared its global headquarters and Asia Pacific engineering hub will remain in Melbourne, creating 200 new local technology jobs.
Tic:Toc will receive SA Government funding of up to $900,000 to deliver an online home loan platform , which is supported by Bendigo and Adelaide Bank.
The Government has announced a review into Australia's space industry capability , which will be developed into a national strategy for the sector to be completed by March 2018. The review will be led by an ‘ Expert Review Group ’ chaired by former CSIRO chief executive Dr Megan Clark AC. South Australia has indicated its unhappiness with the Federal Government’s plan and said the state will ‘look to launch its own structure and organisation’ if the Commonwealth fails to act.

Retail companies selling NBN Sky Muster satellite plans will pass on the extra peak data to customers at 'very little extra cost', according to Minister for Regional Communications Fiona Nash.
A three-year mapping project will investigate the potential power of tidal energy , with $2.5 million invested by the Federal Government through the Australian Renewable Energy Agency. The project is led by the Australian Maritime College at the University of Tasmania in partnership with CSIRO and the University of Queensland.
The formation of matter in the universe is one of the topics to be explored through the new ARC Centre of Excellence for All-sky Astrophysics in 3 Dimensions (ASTRO 3D) based at the Australian National University. It has received $30.3 million over seven years through the ARC Centres of Excellence scheme and will be led by Professor Lisa Kewley.
The Federal Government has released guidelines on the use of section 313(3) of the Telecommunications Act 1997, which enables Government agencies to seek assistance from internet service providers to disrupt websites connected to illegal activity .
An unmanned aerial vehicle that can last five times longer in the air than most drones on the market has been developed by a 19-year-old law student from The University of Western Australia.
Trimble and Tech23 alumni Propeller Aero are officially collaborating on end-to-end drone data workflows.
Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull has announced a Cyber Resilience Taskforce .
Equinix will invest up to $55 million into the expansion of its SY4 Sydney data centre in Alexandria to meet rising demands for better connection services.
The first National Youth Science Forum STEM Explorer program has kicked off today with students from 80 South Australian schools participating.
