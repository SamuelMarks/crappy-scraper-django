NEWS BRIEFS
dgdfgdg Macquarie Telecom (ASX: MAQ) is to implement SD-WAN services at at Spudshed’s retail and farming sites across the state to help it better identify customer needs through real time data analysis, reduce its outage risk and support its business growth.
Australian startup ID Exchange and UK-based firm digi.me have partnered to establish the ID Exchange Innovation Campus a commercial regtech sandbox facility in Western Sydney.
A new science and technology policy for national security has been announced by the Federal Government with new policy priorities including cyber security, intelligence and technology foresighting.
Researchers are creating the first database of the entire cancer spectrum at the Westmead Children’s Medical Research Institute . The NSW Government is investing $21 million in the project, while the Australian Government is contributing $20 million.
The Victorian Government is investing $124 million to roll out electronic medical records across the Peter MacCallum Cancer Centre, Melbourne Health and Royal Women’s Hospital.
Coworking community Tank Stream Labs has brought on entrepreneur and Shark Tank judge Andrew Banks as its entrepreneur in residence .
Provisional State Government funding of $5.5 million will be awarded to the Minerals Research Institute of Western Australia (MRIWA), to support development and manufacturing of technology metals and renewable energy sources. MRIWA will invest the funds, if successful, in its bid to establish a New Energy Industry Cooperative Research Centre (CRC) in WA.
UNSW has opened an analytics lab to convert big data into insights that can aid the creation of smart, resilient and liveable cities.
Australian and French researchers are set to combine efforts around commercialising silicon-based quantum computing technology under a new joint venture .The venture brings together SQC, which is commercialising quantum technology developed out of UNSW, with France’s Alternative Energies and Atomic Energy Commission, known as the CEA.
Rare earth metals miner Hastings Technology Metals (ASX: HAS) wants to use blockchain technology in its supply chain platform to increase the transparency of minerals shipments “from mine to market”.
Australia is set to get its first national Data Commissioner , in part to build public trust in the Government as the custodian of data assets . The commissioner is being pitched as the “trusted overseer of the public data system”, finding ways to “promote greater use of data” while “building trust” in the government’s capabilities in that area.
Blockchain startup Power Ledger has unveiled the first commercial deployment of its energy trading platform in the United States at the Northwestern University Evanston campus, as part of their partnership with the Clean Energy Blockchain Network.

Artchain Global has raised $4 million in seed funding with help from numerous connections in the VC space and has set its sights on the Chinese art and crypto market.
