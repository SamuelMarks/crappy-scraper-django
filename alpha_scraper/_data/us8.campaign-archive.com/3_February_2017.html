NewsBriefs

Seek and Hudson have joined Slingshot ’s HR-focused accelerator Human Capital as corporate partners, with the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) on board to help connect participating startups with its network.
The NSW Government plans to create a new 'supersized' startup hub in Sydney. Business accelerators, incubators and startup partnering services are being asked to sign up to the new Sydney Startup Hub, established by NSW Government-backed Jobs for NSW .
Jobs for NSW has also provided a $120,000 grant to establish the ' EnergyLab ', a startup accelerator program focused on developing NSW's renewable and advanced energy sector. EnergyLab will be based at the Blackfriars School House on the UTS Chippendale campus.
Epworth HealthCare will use Telstra Health's Patient Flow Manager for data visibility across multiple hospital IT systems. The solution will help to plan patient movement and bed flow, which is currently handled manually.
This Valentine's Day the ACCC is warning people to beware of online romance scams - with over $25 million lost in 2016 according to the ACCC's Scamwatch service. Social media is now the most common way for scammers to contact potential victims.
Nominations have opened for the Australian communications industry’s premier awards, the ACOMMS .
An internet-based stuttering clinic has been funded through the National Health and Medical Research Council (NHMRC). The NHMRC grant of $6.2 million was awarded to Professor Mark Onslow at the University of Sydney, which received over $30 million of new NHMRC funding in total. A full list of outcomes for the latest funding round can be found here .
Download the Startup Muster 2016 Report .
80% of broadband consumers are confused about fixed broadband speeds, and want simple, standardised info - according to the ACCC's consultation. In light of this (unsurprising) feedback, the ACCC has published six principles to help ISPs to make sure their claims are not misleading. In response, the Communications Alliance commented: “The principles proposed by the ACCC today... were not discussed with Industry in advance and it is likely that applying the principles will create some practical, commercial and technological challenges that need to be worked through.”
Tyro Payments will offer Afterpay's buy now, pay later product to its in-store merchant base through a new commercial arrangement.
Minister for Police, Lisa Neville has announced a new intelligence system for Victoria Police, developed by SAS Institute Australia. The software system will connect current police databases including LEAP and Interpose.
A new kind of black hole has been discovered: a middleweight or intermediate-mass black hole, which falls outside the known categories of stellar-mass and supermassive black holes. University of Queensland researcher Associate Professor Holger Baumgardt performed computer simulations used in the research project led by Dr Bulent Kiziltan at the Harvard-Smithsonian Centre for Astrophysics.
Optus Business has announced a five-year deal with aged care and community service provider Uniting, to supply Fixed Voice, Mobile, UCaaS, and Data Network services to its network.
Innovation and Science Australia has released its review of Australia’s innovation, science, and research ecosystem. The review has found that Australia has some highly innovative sectors, however most innovations reflect the need for fresh ideas, as most are only new to the business itself.
The Productivity Commission has been called on by ACCAN and SACOSS to further consider affordability issues in the Inquiry on the Telecommunications Universal Service Obligation . The ACCAN has also called on the Federal Government to provide ongoing funding for the Mobile Black Spot Program.
Verizon Australia have opened an Asia-Pacific Advanced Security Operations Centre (SOC) in Canberra, bringing its total SOCs around the world to nine.
mHITs and Vietnamese mobile network operator Viettel have launched a new international mobile remittance corridor between Australia and the mbbank mobile money service in Vietnam.
VMtech and ME bank have entered into a multi-year strategic partnership to deliver a Managed Security Service.
Monash University has received over $18 million to improve the performance of US intelligence analysis using AI. The funding comes from the US Government's Intelligence Advanced Research Projects Activity CREATE program.
Melbourne-based disruptive video technology developer Linius Technologies (ASX: LNU) has been granted additional US patents .
A Senate Inquiry will investigate Centrelink's automated debt collection processes. Submissions are open until 22 March 2017.
GMV, Inmarsat and Lockheed Martin have joined Geoscience Australia's two-year national positioning project . The project is trialling a Satellite Based Augmentation System to improve the accuracy of Australia's positioning.
Drone Complier and the Civil Aviation Safety Authority are developing ' Can I fly there? ', a drone safety app.

PeopleWatch

Intelledox has announced its new CEO, Ray Kiley. Kiley has previously held roles at CoreLogic, Medibank Health Solutions and Telstra. Co-founder and previous CEO Phil Williamson will remain on the Board.
Craig Fulton is now CTO of Aconex Limited (ASX: ACX). Fulton joins Aconex from Telstra, where as Director, Infrastructure and Cloud Solutions, he led the 'Cloud First Strategy'.
David Kagan has been appointed as Chief Operating Officer (COO) at SpeedCast International Limited (ASX: SDA). Kagan was previously COO and President at Globalstar.
The new CEO of Proquest Consulting is Aymeric Zito, with previous CEO Clive Roberts to remain as Sales Director.
Lynwen Connick will be ANZ's new Chief Information Security Officer (CISO). Connick delivered the Government's National Cyber Security Strategy and was previously CISO for the Department of Defence. A video interview is available online here .
