news briefs

A distributed, wireless sensor network with autonomous nodes throughout the Amazon Jungle will continuously monitor wildlife , thanks to an almost $2 million grant by the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation. The four research partners involved in the project, dubbed Providence, are meeting in Australia this week to commence phase one. Dr Alberto Elfes, research scientist at CSIRO’s Data61 is the leader of the Australian arm of Providence, with other partners from Brazil and Spain.
The ATO suffered "its worst unplanned outage in recent memory" last week. The outage was caused by a failure of storage hardware. According to the ATO no data was lost or compromised. The ATO will engage an independent expert to conduct an end-to-end review.
A $500 million Biomedical Translation Fund (BTF) will make venture capital investments in biomedical innovations that have commercialisation potential. BTF Fund Managers include Brandon Capital Partners , OneVentures Management and BioScience Managers .
Mastercard's members will be able to offer B2B clients the ability to make cross-border supplier payments with their existing Mastercards due to a new partnership with Octet Finance , while Octet users will benefit from the integration of Mastercard's payment offerings.
ASIC has released class waivers to give fintech businesses and startups more pathways to test before incurring regulatory costs associated with running their business. The regulatory sandbox has been welcomed by the Australian Government, and will be followed by a second stage legislative sandbox that provides further exemptions for fintech startups.
Vodafone Hutchison Australia is partnering with Dimension Data and FireEye to build a Cyber Defence and Response Centre (CDRC).
The AFP took part in an international law enforcement partnership coordinated by Europol , which last week targeted individuals suspected of initiating denial of service attacks on computer systems.
Mint Payments (ASX: MNW) has signed a two-year licence and distribution agreement with TravelPartner (Aust) Pty Ltd to deliver its online payments processing service.
The Australian Government's $50 million Smart Cities and Suburbs Program is inviting proposals on digital and data community solutions. Applicants can apply for up to $5 million when the first funding round opens next year.
Updater (ASX: UPD) will run a pilot program with Capital One Financial Corporation.
Townsville City Council has signed a $20 million, five-year managed services contract with Optus Business to boost the Council's ICT capabilities.
Construction has started on the STEM Works program in South Australia, with building beginning at the first of 139 schools receiving infrastructure overhauls.
CSIRO aims to bring to market its PhotonAssay X-ray gold detection technology through a partnership with Chrysos Corporation .
Aconex Limited (ASX: ACX) is partnering with Fletcher Building Limited (ASX: FBU) on a three-year enterprise agreement.
Draft legislation released by the Federal Government includes provisions for the establishment of the Regional Broadband Scheme to fund long-term costs of regional and remote Australia's NBN networks.
The NSW Government is looking for innovative ideas by the beginning of February 2017 on energy storage technology and will provide support to the best proposals.
Queensland’s Department of Environment and Heritage Protection has engaged CSIRO scientists to attach satellite tracking devices to little red flying-foxes to improve management of roosts in urban areas.
YPB Group Limited (ASX: YPB) has signed a three-year contract with a major US-based pharmaceutical company. The company will use YPB's PROTECT offering with a focus on forensic laboratory services and IP solution design and implementation.
NAB is launching an Application Programming Interface (API) Developer Portal , which will go live before Christmas. The portal will make some NAB APIs publicly available for third party developers to connect to sets of NAB data.
VicRoads will seek industry feedback on the Victorian Government's Future Directions Paper , which considers regulatory changes to allow road testing of highly automated vehicles.
The Federal Court has ordered internet companies to block The Pirate Bay and four other bittorrent websites. This is the first time site-blocking laws have been successfully used in Australia, with Foxtel and Village-Roadshow welcoming the decision.
Adelaide Zoo is the first zoo in Australia to deploy Apple’s iBeacon interactive technology to enhance visitor experience.
dorsaVi (ASX: DVL) has been contracted to provide ViSafe services to Heathrow Airport, in a 12-month contract to be delivered in three phases. dorsaVi will deliver ViSafe Comparison Assessments to compare the safety of two manual handling aids to assist baggage handlers. dorsaVi's motion analysis device technologies measure the musculoskeletal impact of using manual handling aids.
REA Group's (ASX: REA) realestate.com.au has entered a five-year strategic partnership with National Australia Bank (ASX: NAB) to create an 'integrated digital home loan experience' on their website.
A Digital Roadmap , including a new-look MyGov, has been released. The Roadmap sets out projects and timelines for digital government services in 2017.

people watch

Kate McKenzie is the new CEO of Chorus (ASX: CNU). She will commence the role in February 2017. McKenzie was most recently Telstra's COO.
Former Head of Security for Atlassian, Craig Davies , is to be CEO of the Cyber Security Growth Centre. The role will commence in early 2017.
Thomas Anbeek has left his role as Head of Business Operations at Startup Victoria, and is now Director at Roshambo and Entrepreneur in Residence at Victoria University .
Dr Helen Cleugh is the first Director of the CSIRO Climate Science Centre.
Sage has moved its Australian headquarters to a new facility in Chatswood Sydney, and appointed Natalie Chrara - previously with Salesforce.com - as Vice President, ANZ Sales. Varun Batra also joins Sage as Director - Professional Services.
The Northern Territory's renewable energy panel have met to further plans to deliver a Roadmap to Renewables Report in 2017. The panel is chaired by Alan Langworthy, Managing Director of Langworthy NT Pty Ltd.
Bob Mansfield AO will join the Vocus Board (ASX: VOC) as a Non-Executive Director. Mansfield was the founding CEO of Optus Communications and was appointed Director of Telstra Corporation Limited in 1999 and served as Chairman for four years.
Steven Worrall is Microsoft's Australian Managing Director. He replaces Pip Marlow, who will take up the role of CEO, Strategic Innovation at Suncorp.

company watch

Skyfii (ASX: SKF) has raised more than $4.1 million in a funding round from new and existing investors. Existing shareholders provided the majority of the funding, including Jan Cameron (Founder, Kathmandu) and the White family of the Ray White real estate group. New investors include Peter Gammell (Chairman, Octet Finance) and former Seven Group and Foxtel CEO Richard Freudenstein.
Freelancer.com has acquired Spanish and Portuguese freelancing marketplaces Nubelo and Prolancer . The terms of the acquisition were not disclosed.
APN Outdoor Group Limited (ASX: APO) and oOh!media Limited (ASX: OML) are to merge via an all-scrip scheme of arrangement. APN Outdoor and oOh!media shareholders will own 55% and 45% of the Merged Group respectively. Richard Herring, who has been CEO of APN Outdoor Media for 16 years and Peter McClelland, CFO and COO of oOh!media have stood aside to facilitate the merger. The Merged Group will be headed up by - Brendon Cook as CEO; Wayne Castle as CFO and Doug Flynn as Chairman.
Fantasy Sports Global has acquired wagering and gaming technology company Favourit Australia Pty Ltd .
