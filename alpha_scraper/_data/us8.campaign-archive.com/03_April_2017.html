NewsBriefs

Four Australian banks (Commonwealth, Westpac, NAB, Bendigo & Adelaide) have been denied authorisation to collectively bargain with Apple and boycott Apple Pay . The banks wanted to bargain with Apple for access to the Near-Field Communication controller in iPhones, and reasonable access terms to the App Store.
Discovery platform Taboola is opening a new regional office in Sydney and, in New Zealand, has signed a premium publisher partnership with Newshub at Mediaworks .
A new CSIRO initiative provides dollar-matched funding up to $50,000 for startup and small business research projects. One of the first recipients of CSIRO Kick-Start is Tomcar Australia, who is using the funding for feasibility research into electrification of their modified off-grid, off-road vehicles with CSIRO's Manufacturing team.
A fleet of autonomous robots is set to start delivering pizzas under a pilot program announced by Domino's and Starship Technologies . The DRU (Domino's Robotic Unit) will use robot and drone technologies for delivery in selected German and Dutch cities.
Curtin University is trialling a commercial driverless bus , built by French-based company Navya.
SaaS solution company GetSwift Limited (ASX: GSW) has signed an exclusive contract with London's Crosstown Doughnuts .
The ACCC has issued a draft decision to reject nbn's proposed variation to its Special Access Undertaking to incorporate new tech such as FTTN, FTTB and HFC.
The University of Queensland has launched new co-working space the UQ IdeaHub. The Director of the UQ IdeaHub is Nimrod Klayman.
This year Telstra's Innovation Challenge will focus on Australian agriculture . Teams will design, hack and code an IoT prototype solution to solve one of the challenges faced by Australian farmers. Telstra has also released a white paper on 'Regional Australia's Technology Future'.
The Victorian Government held a digital roundtable to discuss the benefits of new tech for the agriculture sector .
Patients recovering from total knee replacements will be able to use a new smartphone app developed by CSIRO and Johnson & Johnson Medical Devices to provide them with practical pre- and post-surgery information, as well as a wearable activity tracker to encourage basic exercise and self-monitor progress.
The creative industries in Australia now have their first startup accelerator with QUT Creative Enterprise Australia's launch of the Collider Accelerator program . 10 early stage creative tech startups will receive $20,000 in seed capital, opportunities for business growth, access to mentors and working space.
Families with a loved one who has suffered a traumatic brain injury are invited to take part in a 10 week online communication training program. Run by the University of Sydney, the clinical trial aims to assess whether online video applications like Skype can provide better access to services for people with traumatic brain injury.
ikeGPS (ASX: IKE) has signed a marketing and software integration deal with SA International and its Flexi software.

PeopleWatch

Chris Ridd , former Managing Director, Australia for Xero, is now CEO of myprosperity.
Vocus (ASX: VOC) has appointed Michael Simmons as Chief Executive Enterprise and Wholesale Australia.
The Office of the Queensland Chief Entrepreneur has collaborated with Ben Southall to create the ' Venturer ' program to inspire and train the state's entrepreneurial talent. The 'adventurer-in-residence' will work with entrepreneurs to 'take them out of their comfort zone'.
Big Switch Networks has appointed Steve Coad as Managing Director, Australia and New Zealand. Coad was most recently Aruba's Managing Director, South Pacific.
MNF Group (ASX: MNF) has appointed Monica Singireddy as Software Team Manager. She was previously with Philips Dynalite.
Andrew Jaspan , Editor and Executive Director of The Conversation, is leaving the organisation for RMIT, where he will take up the role of Director of the Global Academy and Professorial Fellowship.

CompanyWatch

Vocus (ASX: VOC) has sold its shares (approximately 16%) in Macquarie Telecom (ASX: MAQ).
Edutech startup 1Scope has raised $1 million in funding from Chinese-backed property developer Aqualand.
The Federal Government is investing $80 million in the $236 million 113 MW Bodangora wind farm project through the Clean Energy Finance Corporation. Funds from Infigen Energy’s (ASX: IFN) fully underwritten capital raising of around $151 million will be used to implement the company's development of wind and solar opportunities, including financing the equity component of Bodangora wind farm.
