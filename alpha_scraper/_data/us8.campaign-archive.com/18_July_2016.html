news briefs

We may be one step closer to practical jetpacks , with Martin Aircraft Company (ASX: MJP) signing an Alliance Agreement with US company Avwatch Incorporated. The companies will develop specific capabilities for the North American First Responder and Homeland Security sectors, key markets for the First Responder Martin Jetpack. The agreement creates a platform to seek R&D funding for the development of the Martin Jetpack to meet US first responder requirements. Subject to certain criteria being met, Avwatch plans to purchase manned and unmanned jetpacks.
KPMG has launched corporate fintech accelerator mLabs , to connect Authorised Deposit-taking Institutions with Australian fintech startups. The program will be used by Beyond Bank, CUA, Greater Bank, Heritage, IMB Bank, Police Bank, and Teachers Mutual Bank. 14 fintech startups have been selected for the first iteration of the mLabs accelerator, including: Avoka ; Brighte ; Chekk ; Cloudcase ; DSYNC ; Easyshare ; Edstart ; Fitchain; Flamingo ; Moneycatcha ; Moroku ; Pocketbook ; Simplekyc ; and Spriggy .
ANZ customers can now use Android Pay to make contactless payments . Android Pay uses tokenisation security, which generates a unique number for each purchase, ensuring customer details are not shared directly with the retailer.
Airtasker has partnered with The Good Guys to provide a new installation platform which connects customers with the Good Guys' network of installers. Airtasker co-founder and COO Jonathan Lui said that he expected the platform to 'trigger a fundamental shift in how retailers think about the post-purchase customer experience'.
Milestone Sports Limited will partner with the La Trobe Sport and Exercise Medicine Research Centre in Melbourne to assess the effects of injury risk factors and beginner running programs on the body. The project will use Milestone's MilestonePod , which tracks detailed gait metrics through sensor technology, enabling researchers to gather data from real runs rather than in controlled lab environments. Victory Mines Limited (ASX: VIC) has a binding Heads of Agreement to acquire 100% of the issued capital of Milestone Sports Limited, which is to be renamed Milestone Connex.
nbn has signed six new delivery partners - Lend Lease, Broadspectrum, Fulton Hogan, Downer, ISGM and BSA.
We are a step closer to using renewable or ambient energy from mobile phone base stations to power battery-operated wireless sensors in industries like health and agriculture. ANU researchers have modelled how much energy it takes to sense and transfer information by wireless sensors.
NetComm Wireless Limited (ASX: NTC) has signed an agreement to supply fixed wireless devices to Nokia. Under the agreement, NetComm Wireless will provide Nokia with Wireless Network Terminating Devices engineered to deliver LTE broadband in rural and regional areas worldwide in specific frequencies.
ISGM and Foxtel will vary an existing contract to manage customer installations and maintenance of satellite and Cable TV, commercial and MDU work, and broadband services. The varied contract will run for an initial term of three years with the option to extend.
Boppl is partnering with US fast-food chain Larry's Giant Subs and Clover point-of-sale systems. Boppl designed and developed iPhone and Android mobile applications for customer orders and payment. Yonder & Beyond (ASX: YNB) has 72% interest in Boppl.
Digisoft has signed an extended agreement with Linius, which will position Digisoft as the first reseller of the Linius technology.

company watch

Catapult Group (ASX: CAT) has acquired video-based technology company XOS Digital for $80.1 million and Ireland-based GPS-tracking technology for amateur athletes, PlayerTek, for $3.6 million. Catapult is launching a $100 million raising fully underwritten by Goldman Sachs.
Telstra is to acquire enterprise mobility solutions provider MSC Mobility. MSC has partnered with Telstra for a decade.
Chapmans (ASX:CHP) subsidiary Chapmans Opportunities Limited (COL) has invested $600,000 or a 19% equity holding in plasma gasification waste-to-energy business Syn Dynamics Australia Pty Ltd. COL may increase its interest to 25% for another $1.9 million following the completion of a CSIRO report. Plasma gasification converts carbon-based material to synthetic gas or 'syngas', which can be used for renewable energy and fuel. Chapmans has also decided not to proceed with its proposed transactions with Skyfii Limited (ASX: SKF) and Whizz Technologies Pty Ltd, citing 'declining valuations and investor sentiment in the cloud-based digital technology sector'.
Atlassian has acquired StatusPage. StatusPage already powers status.atlassian.com , status.bitbucket.org , and status.hipchat.com . StatusPage enables companies to create a hosted status page for communicating updates on technology services to their customers.
Melbourne-based Nura has raised more than US$1.8 million from over 7700 backers through its Kickstarter compaign for its in-ear and over-ear headphones that automatically tune to you.

people watch

John Thompson has been appointed CEO of Knosys (ASX: KNO). Thompson was previously CEO of Sigtec, and prior to that was CEO of Wavenet International.
Brian Donn has been announced as Head of CX, Cloud Applications Business, APAC at Oracle . Donn was previously with Verint systems.
Isaac Young is SuiteBox 's new Chief Technology Officer. Young has previously worked in financial services technology for UBS, Rabobank and Commerzbank.
