NewsBriefs

The University of Queensland’s iLab has announced its cohort of 10 startups to participate in the three-month Germinate accelerator program.
Veriluma (ASX: VRI), is to develop an ‘ Indicators and Warnings ’ model for climate and disaster preparedness for the Australian Government’s Department of Defence .
Trident Subsea Cable has fast-tracked its plan for connectivity between Indonesia and Singapore through a partnership with Triasmitra. The connectivity plan is a part of the broader telecommunications network between Asia and Australia.
Thinxtra has announced a new partnership program with the University of Technology Sydney to provide free Internet of Things​ (IoT) connectivity to the Faculty of Engineering and IT to assist R&D projects.
The Mexican Armed Forces has ordered a supply of Vital Record Documents from brand protection company YPB Group Limited 's (ASX: YPB) joint venture with Affyrmx LLC.
Driverless cars could be a thing of the very near future in Victoria with the Victorian Government - in partnership with Australian Road Research Board and La Trobe University - set to test semi-automated vehicles on the Eastlink Motorway as part of an 18-month trial.
A partnership between LifeQ and Medibio (ASX: MEB) aims to deliver mental wellness diagnostic and management solutions through insurance and corporate programs. LifeQ's systems biology modelling and information platform will be combined with Medibio's mental health research.
The University of Melbourne , with the support of the Victorian Government , will begin a ‘ National Connected Multimodal Transport Test Bed ’ focused on reducing future road congestion and improving safety.
Skyfii (ASX: SKF) and Optus Business are expanding guest WiFi and consumer analytics services across five Mirvac retail shopping centre sites.
The design and build of the US$2.6 billion Sheikh Jaber Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah Causeway in Kuwait will use Aconex (ASX: ACX) solutions. The Kuwait Ministry of Public Works' project will connect Kuwait City with an area across Kuwait Bay known as the 'City of Silk'.
Perhaps unsurprisingly, research commissioned by the nbn has shown that regional Australians are more likely to use their nbn connection to manage their health conditions. 5% of consumers surveyed nationally have consulted a health practitioner online.
However, the Australian Medical Association (AMA) has recently called on the Government to provide better access to high-speed broadband for rural and remote Australia. Read the AMA's full position statement here .
In more remote health news, a telehealth solution will be made available to Australians in regional and remote areas through an alliance between Health Team Australia and Coviu , a CSIRO Data61 project.
The Victorian Government has opened a new monitoring hub for the Victorian police . The surveillance centre will give police real-time access to information from across all departments. The hub will be monitored 24/7 by police, with science, tech, engineering and mathematical analysts to help interpret the data. In correlation with the centre, the Victorian Government is set to increase its tech initiative by giving police new mobile devices, body-worn cameras and intelligence and analysis systems. Highway patrol cars will also be fitted with new automated number plate recognition technology.
The ACCC has released its draft decision for the declared superfast broadband access service and local bitstream access service. The decision includes prices that retail service providers will pay to buy NBN-like fixed line superfast broadband wholesale services on non-NBN networks.
Robo 3D Limited (ASX: RBO) has chosen Hon Hai Precision Industry (trading as Foxconn Technology Group) as its production partner for the company's Robo R2 smart 3D printer .
Techstars will open its first Asia-Pacific based accelerator in Australia - Techstars Adelaide . The Accelerator Program will support early stage companies in IoT, big data, sensors, and robotics.

PeopleWatch

Qantas (ASX: QAN) has appointed Susan Doniz as the company’s new Chief Information Officer.
Complexica 's new Corporate Strategy Advisor is Doug Misener, previously CEO of Liquor Marketing Group.
Covata Limited (by Tech23 alumni Cocoon Data) (ASX: CVT) has appointed Ted Pretty as Managing Director and CEO.

CompanyWatch

REA Group (ASX: REA) will acquire a 14.7%, $67.9 million strategic stake in Indian digital real estate marketing platform, PropTiger.
Atlassian is to acquire project management service Trello for US$425 million, comprising around US$360 million in cash and the remainder in Atlassian restricted shares, restricted share units and options to acquire Atlassian shares. Trello will continue to operate as a standalone service.
Tech23 alumni Plutora and winner of the Tech23 2016 Greatest Potential Award has closed a $13.4 million growth equity investment from Macquarie Capital. The funds will support international growth.
All in Media , Cicada Innovations ' portfolio company, has been acquired by DTS, Inc . Cicada Innovations has also announced new portfolio company Repartech.
H2 Ventures has been given a $2 million loan guarantee by the NSW Government’s Department of Industry to access a $4 million loan by Investec Australia Limited.
Telecommunications provider United Networks (ASX: UNL) has listed on the ASX . The company's IPO raised just over $7 million.
