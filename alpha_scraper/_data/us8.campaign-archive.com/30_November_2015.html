news briefs

Commercialising new technologies developed by University of South Australia's Institute for Telecommunications Research which was supported by the Australian Space Research Program, Myriota Pty Ltd will use low earth orbit satellites to provide two-way data connectivity for remote sensors and devices. The lead investor and commercial partner for Myriota is Canadian company, exactEarth Ltd , who are providing $2 million to commercialise the new technology.
Congratulations to the organisers and participants of TechFugees Australia Hackathon which developed tech solutions for refugee settlement challenges articulated by Settlement Services International and STARTTS over the weekend. Hosted by NSW’s Liverpool City Council, the Winning team was GoalifyOz which created an app to enable refugees to track their progress transitioning into Australian life.
Orion Health 's (ASX: OHE) Rhapsody integration software, in a partnership with Fujitsu Australia has been selected to replace Queensland Health's legacy middleware system in a first step in a large program of work which will incrementally replace Queensland Health's IT infrastructure.
Victorian Minister for Small Business, Innovation and Trade Philip Dalidakis has announced the launch of LaunchVic, a new independent body to administer the long-promised $60 million startup fund to be provided over four years.
According to a new report, there is clear link between well-developed national innovation policy and the economic performance of a country. The report released by the Australian Council of Learned Academies concluded Australia was squandering opportunity. Amongst its 15 key findings - the idea of incentivising business to collaborate with public sector researchers; increasing the support to enable commercialisation of outcomes from public sector research; and the establishment of a national strategy and an independent agency to manage it.
AIMIA has closed its doors, calling in the administrators. AIMIA has represented the digital media industry in Australia since 2002. It is understood that merger talks with an aligned industry association held over recent months have broken down. Members will vote before Christmas on the association’s future.
There is to be a further round of consultation on the Telecommunications and Other Legislation Amendment Bill . Submissions on the exposure draft will close on Monday 18 January 2016.
Lockheed Martin Australia has officially opened its Submarine Combat Systems Laboratory in collaboration with Saab Australia and Thales Australia in Mawson Lakes, South Australia.
TAFE South Australia has partnered with Microsoft to shift its online learning system into Microsoft 's Azure cloud.
Congratulations to Jessica May, Founder of Enabled Employment for winning the Start-Up Award at the National Telstra Business Women's Awards .
Congratulations to QuintessenceLabs for winning the international Security Innovation Network Award .
Empired (ASX: EPT) has secured a five year contract with Horizon Power worth $10 million. Under the contract, EPT will provide a range of managed services for Horizon's ICT infrastructure assets across Western Australia.
Communications Alliance has released for public comment a series of proposed revisions to Chapter 9 of the Telecommunications Consumer Protection Code 2012. The public comment period will run until Wednesday 30 December 2015.

company watch

Tyro Payments Limited has raised $100 million from Tiger Global Management in New York, TDM Asset Management and Australian technology entrepreneur Mike Cannon-Brookes to fund the next stage of its growth.
SurfStitch Group (ASX: SRF) is to acquire water board sports product company, SHI for $23.7 million.
Superloop Limited (ASX: SLC) has acquired CINENET Systems Pty Ltd for $30 million, consisting of $1.5 million in cash and $1.5 million in Superloop scrip.
UXC Limited (ASX: UXC) has entered into a Scheme Implementation Deed with Computer Sciences Corporation , to acquire 100% of UXC shares for a cash consideration of $1.22 per share.
VentureCrowd has confirmed $1 million has been raised from a small number of cornerstone investors, including several investing in the technology sector for the first time.
Hobart based startup Biteable has raised $1.1 million in seed funding from Australian investors including Tank Stream Ventures and BridgeLane Capital .

people watch

The Australian Computer Society has elected Anthony Wong as its president for a two year term commencing in January 2016.
Lucy Turnbull AO has been appointed patron of not-for-profit organisation Digital, Innovative, Creative and Entrepreneurial Kids , an organisation that aims to foster entrepreneurialism among 180,000 Australian children with a National Lemonade Day.
