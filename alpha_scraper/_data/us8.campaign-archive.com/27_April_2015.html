news briefs

StartupAus has released its updated report Crossroads which documents "where we need to be". "StartupAus is concerned that Australia's fledging startup ecosystem is falling further behind in an international context, and that without urgent action from the government it will become increasingly difficult for Australia to transition to a knowledge economy in which startups play an important role''. The full list of StartupAUS Crossroads 2015 Report recommendations is: • Create a national innovation agency • Increase the number of entrepreneurs • Improve the quality and quantity of entrepreneurship education • Increase the number of people with ICT skills • Improve access to startup expertise • Increase availability of early stage capital to startups • Address legal and regulatory impediments • Increase collaboration and international connectedness.
Sustainability Victoria has released an Invitation for Expressions of Interest from parties to own, maintain and distribute the FirstRate5 House Energy Rating Software. It is seeking a party who is committed to its ongoing development and the support of users.
More than 800 entries from the Foundation for Young Australians (FYA) Innovation Nation campaign have been whittled down to the 50 participants chosen for the Young Social Pioneers program to receive mentorship and a shot at seed money to make their ideas a reality.
PHW Consolidated Limited (ASX:PHW), which is poised to acquire Tomizone Limited , has entered into partnership with STQRY . STQRY's technology offers location aware customised content built on Tomizone's Wi_Fi software platform.
Newzulu Limited (ASX:NWZ) has entered into a strategic partnership and content syndication agreement with Getty Images which will see the creation of the Newzulu Collection on gettyimages.com .
Victoria's Auditor-General John Doyle has tabled a report in Parliament indicating that in 2011–12, 2012–13 and 2013–14, the average annual ICT expenditure of Victorian government agencies and entities was $3.02 billion, which is 4.3% of the average annual state operating expenditure of $71.04 billion. It concluded that Victorian government agencies and entities are currently not only unable to demonstrate the achievement of expected benefits from ICT investments, they are also, in general, unable to comprehensively report actual ICT expenditure, or the status of projects.
Domain name registrar auDA is welcoming comments in regards to whether it should allow second level direct registration of domain names under .au – something that is available in other countries.
Researchers at CSIRO , QIMR Berghofer Medical Research Institute and the Australian National University are working on developing very specific, sensitive and cheap biosensors to replace traditional blood tests for malaria with breath tests by identifying chemicals that can be detected in the breath of patients infected with the disease. The study, published in the Journal of Infectious Diseases can be viewed here .
Android TV is now available in Australian retail outlets, JB Hi-Fi and Dick Smith — starting with the Nexus Player, a collaboration with Asus .
Adult Retrieval Victoria , a part of Ambulance Victoria , is adopting two new systems developed by Readify . The Adult Retrieval Victoria Information System and the Retrieval and Critical Health Information System will be used by the department to obtain real time data, assisting them in treating critically ill patients.
The Australian Communications Consumer Action Network has made a submission on the Copyright Amendment (Online Infringement) Bill 2015, calling on the Government to remove ambiguity around Virtual Private Networks and subject the scheme to a cost benefit analysis.
NBN Co has dropped the ‘Co’ from its name rebranding itself with a lowercase ‘nbn’ surrounded by a blue map of Australia. The company has also adopted the new motto, ‘Bring it on’.
NAB has partnered with Xero to integrate its all-cloud accounting environment to its Application Programming Interface to enable users to create feeds online within the bank’s internet banking platform.
The Social Innovation Entrepreneurship and Enterprise Alliance have launched a manifesto for social enterprises.
That Startup Show has launched on BitTorrent as a BitTorrent Bundle, with the first two pilot episodes free to download and all six episodes available for purchase for $3.82.
Entries for The Young ICT Explorers 2015 competition, for school years 3 - 12, is now open. Students can submit any project with a clear link to ICT that they have been working on inside or outside the classroom. Entries can be from individuals or teams of up to four. See all guidelines here .

company watch

Atlassian has acquired Blue Jimp , creators of an open source videoconferencing technology. Atlassian hopes to expand it’s HipChat video capabilities through the acquisition.
Alcidion Corporation has raised $1.5 million in Series B funding led by Blue Sky Private Equity , Allure Capital and Alicidion Co-founders Ray Blight and Malcolm Pradhan. Alcidion previously received $2.4 million in 2012 by a fund managed by Blue Sky Private Equity, which helped the company fund the development of its support platform.
Singtel (ASX: SGT) is to delist from the ASX in early June.
Sendle , a spin off from goods swap site Tushare founded by James Chin Moody, has closed $1.8 million in funding led by The National Roads and Motorist’s Association (NRMA). NRMA, which also backed Tushare, contributed $1 million with the rest of the funds received by high net-worth individuals. The company plans to use the funds to consolidate regional and rural Australia and improve its software platform.
Australian charity Shout for Good has been acquired by the ANZ Bank for an undisclosed amount. The acquisition follows Shout for Good’s $1 million raise last year in order to expand into the US market.
My Net Fone (ASX: MNF) has completed its acquisition of Telecom New Zealand International for $22 million.
Freelancer Limited (ASX: FLN) is to acquire Escrow.com for US$7.5 million. Escrow.com is headquarted in California and acts as a trusted third party in online transaction management. A capital raising of $10 million to institutional investors has been oversubscribed which will help fund the acquisition.
Ingogo has announced plans to raise between $3-$4 million through VentureCrowd , which raised $1.2 million for the company last year as part of a broader $9.3 million funding round. The raise is expected to close in May.
iiNet (ASX: IIN) has confirmed that it received an acquisition proposal from M2 Group Limited (ASX: MTU). M2 is offering 0.803 M2 shares plus a 75 cent dividend for each iiNet share which would see iiNet shareholders owning approximately 42% of the enlarged M2 Group. This follows the recent acquistition proposal by TPG Telecom .
Sydney-based crowdfunding startup StartSomeGood has raised $400,000, led by Trimantium Capital ’s serial entrepreneur Phillip Kingston.
1stAvailable has lodged a prospectus with ASIC to list on the ASX at the end of May to raise $10 million. 1stAvailable was established in 2009 and since April 2012 has been providing online search and appointment booking service for participating private healthcare providers. After listing, 1stAvailable plans to acquire GObookings , Clinic Connect and DocAppointments who all have complementary businesses.
