news briefs

Innovation Central Sydney will focus on Internet of Things innovation and be housed at deep technology incubator, ATP Innovations. Founding partners include Cisco, Data61, UNSW, National Farmers' Federation, NSW Farmers, Primary Industries, Lands and Water.
A sonar technology trial for shark detection has commenced at Bondi Beach, as part of a $16 million Shark Management Strategy. 'Clever Buoy' uses sonar imaging technology deployed on the seabed to detect a shark's distinct movements. Real-time data will be made available via the SharkSmart app .
The community group Newstead 2021 has signed a memorandum of understanding with Powercor Australia to move Newstead towards becoming the first Victorian town powered by 100% locally generated renewable energy.
Synroc, an Australian innovation which stores nuclear waste, will deliver a fully integrated processing plant to treat the byproduct of Mo-99 (molybdenum-99), a radioisotope widely used in the diagnosis of cancer, cardiac conditions and skeletal injuries. The Synroc manufacturing facility was funded by the Federal Government as part of the Australian Nuclear Science and Technology Organisation (ANSTO), and will treat intermediate level waste from the ANSTO Nuclear Medicine project.
Satellite data streaming services have been introduced to the Spirit of Tasmania through a wholesale partnership between Optus Satellite and Electrotech Australia. The technology will provide service delivery for enterprise use as well as internet access for passengers.
The Energy Networks Association (ENA) and the Australian Renewable Energy Agency (ARENA) have released an updated database of Australian and international renewable energy grid integration projects. The Integrating Renewables into the Grid Stocktake catalogues 208 projects, with the aim of enhancing 'collective knowledge and experience of integrating renewable energy into distribution networks'. The Stocktake will provide information on current projects and outcomes of past efforts to integrate renewables with electricity grids.
CSIRO Chairman David Thodey has published a response to concerns regarding proposed cuts to Australian climate research.
The Benevolent Society has awarded an IT contract to Enablis , who will take on the charity's fixed voice and data services. Enablis will deliver WAN, LAN, Wifi and Unified Voice services across 55 sites in NSW and Queensland, and will also upgrade call centre operations.
realcommercial.com.au is using its News Corp connections to increase their reach through the addition of News Corp Australia's mastheads, and The Wall Street Journal Asia.
Charity Whitelion is asking people to 'lock out' of social media for 48 hours to raise money and awareness for disadvantaged and at-risk Australian youth.
A 4 Tesla magnet from an MRI machine at the University of Queensland's Centre for Advanced Imaging will be used as part of an experiment investigating the foundations of the universe. The magnet will be installed at CERN in Switzerland, home of the Large Hadron Collider.
A new app, Harlie , is designed to help people with neurological conditions to practise speech. Users can converse with the chat-bot, which analyses aspects of the speaker's voice and communication, including articulation of vowels, vocabulary, and pauses. The app is designed by researchers at CSIRO and University of Queensland. The general public is invited to chat to Harlie so that it can be better prepared for more clinical studies.
Water Corporation of Western Australia has launched Interactive Intelligence Group 's omnichannel customer engagement software suite.
Optus has launched a new Future Makers program to 'foster digital innovation' and support entrepreneurs, social enterprises and not-for-profits. An accelerator program will be provided for 12 shortlisted candidates, with six winners then selected to receive up to $50,000. Ideas need to centre on employability, education, cyber education and safety, or wellbeing.
The University of Western Australia 's Faculty of Science has announced the arrival of 'Pople', a high-performance computing cluster. The supercomputer will support advanced research in computational chemistry, biology and physics.
WA Health Deputy Director General Rebecca Brown last week accepted the findings and recommendations in the Office of the Auditor General's report into WA Health's Centralised Community Services contract with Fujitsu. Specific initiatives undertaken by WA Health from 2014 include the overhaul of ICT governance across the organisation.
CottonInfo has launched a Birds on Cotton Farms app to help users identify birds. It is based on the original 'Birds on Cotton Farms' book. The app features bird call sounds and a monitoring tool for keeping track of bird populations. CottonInfo is supported by CRDC, Cotton Australia and CSD.

company watch

Waratah Resources (ASX: WGO) has entered into a non-binding letter of intent with start-up generator Investible . Waratah are prioritising commitment to initial technology investment with the assistance of Tulla Technology Pty Ltd.
VTX Holdings Limited (ASX: VTX) - to be renamed Wangle Technologies - has acquired 100% of the A Class Shares in NexGen Networks Limited. Jason Gitmans and Keaton Wallace have been appointed as Directors, while James Robinson and Harry Karelis have resigned.
Movio - a Vista Group International company (ASX: VGL) headquartered in New Zealand - and Vue Entertainment have entered a multi-year agreement to deploy Movio Cinema in 84 theatres in the UK and Ireland. Movio Cinema enables cinema exhibitors to analyse their data and execute personalised campaigns. It also integrates with the cinema management system Vista Cinema.
Digital Star Media Holdings Pty Limited has entered into an agreement to raise $3 million for business development. The company is developing an app-based sports fan engagement business to be launched later in 2016. Chapmans Limited (ASX: CHP) has a 40% direct holding in Digital Star Media, valued at $4.8 million. Digital Star Media has entered into a mandate with Lodge Corporate Pty Ltd, in which Lodge will act as Corporate Advisor and Lead Manager for a pre-IPO capital raise.

people watch

StartupAUS has named Alex McCauley as the organisation's new CEO. McCauley was previously Head of Strategy and Advocacy at StartupAUS, and had formerly held a diplomatic position in Israel. Founding Director and Interim CEO Peter Bradd will stay on as Chairman.
Philip Milne has been named CEO UXC Red Rock , effective from March 2016. Milne joined UXC Red Rock 10 years ago as the co-founder and CEO of BML & Associates, which was then acquired by UXC Red Rock.
Mani Thiru has been appointed as Senior Director, Alliances at Marketo . Thiru is based in Sydney.
