news briefs

Babysitting app Kindy has won a US $100,000 convertible note from Qualcomm Ventures at the 2014-15 OzApp Awards .
AngelCube is opening for its 2015 intake. In exchange for 10% equity, the Melbourne accelerator provides companies $20,000 in seed capital, six months of office space, 13 weeks of training and access to a network of mentors and partners in Australia and the US.
Ziptel (ASX: ZIP) has entered into a Memorandum of Understanding with Speedcast International Limited (ASX: SDA) for an exclusive white label application. Under the agreement SDA will white label ZipT application and platforms, which will be re-branded under the SDA’s ‘SpeedTalk’ portfolio of products and services.
The Victorian Government has signed a $41.5 million agreement with Motorola Solutions to extend the management of Victoria’s Metropolitan Data Network (MDN) for an additional two years from March 2015, with an option for two additional one year extensions. MDN was first deployed in 2005 and provides secure data communications for Victoria Police and Ambulance Victoria.
Vocus Communications (ASX: VOC) has entered into a US $58.5 million purchase program for additional capacity on the Southern Cross Cable which connects Australia and New Zealand to the US. The deal would see capacity allocated and paid in annual instalments over a six year term, starting in December this year.
Communications Alliance has published a new draft industry Code of Practice for public comment to drive down the rate of online copyright infringement in Australia. The Code creates a Copyright Notice Scheme which residential internet users who are alleged to have infringed copyright online will receive a series of infringement notices designed to change their behaviour and steer them toward lawful sources of content.
Grants of $10,000-$60,000 for projects that undertake research on telecoms issues, represent telco consumers or create educational tools are available from the Australian Communications Consumer Action Network with applications closing on 31 March 2015.
All eligible residents and service providers in New South Wales can now register a domain name ending in .sydney.
Nominations are now open for the prestigious Communications Alliance and CommsDay annual ACOMM Awards. If your company is doing great things in customer service, community initiatives, security, professional services, marketing, mobile, satellite, or innovative products or services - be sure to put your hand up to be recognised. Submissions close 17 April 2015.
The Attorney General George Brandis has launched a 'Combating Terrorist Propaganda in Australia' scheme which aims to limit the influence of extremist messages on social media. $18 million has been allocated to the scheme.
Greens Senator Scott Ludlam has criticised the Federal Government's proposed mandatory data retention scheme, saying there is no proof that data retention improves safety or reduces crime.
As part of a pilot program developed by NSW Police Strategic Technologies & Planning and Business & Technology Services, 500 Samsung Galaxy smart phones have been deployed to the NSW Police Force .
Presto has expanded its broadcasting compatibility to smart phones. Presto TV will be a 50-50 joint venture between Foxtel and Seven West Media, subject to ACCC approval.

company watch

Local services marketplace, Oneflare has acquired Word Of Mouth Online (WOMO) for an undisclosed amount. Both companies will maintain separate profiles and brands will continue to operate independently.

people watch

Telstra CEO David Thodey has announced his retirement after almost six years in the position. He will be replaced by Telstra’s current Chief Financial Officer Andrew Penn effective 1 May 2015.
Emerchants (ASX: EML) has appointed Peter Martin as Chairman to replace Bob Browning who has stepped down from the position but will remain on the company’s board.
Amir Nissen is the Program Manager for AngelCube . Nissen was previously involved with the Melbourne Accelerator Program (MAP) at the University of Melbourne, as well as participating in Startmate as a founder in their 2013 intake.
