news briefs

The Industry Innovation and Competitiveness Agenda has been released by the Federal Government. Initiatives include encouraging employee share ownership; accepting international standards and risk assessments for certain product approvals; enhancing the 457 and investor visa programmes and establishing Industry Growth Centres. The Industry Growth Centres will aim to improve collaboration between businesses, scientists and researchers and increase the commercialisation of new ideas. Funding will be available to the Centres to develop and deliver large scale collaborative projects focusing on market, value chain or technology issues to deliver commercial outcomes.
A new information technology platform will be established to support the Industry and Competitiveness Agenda . The Industry Growth Network will provide infrastructure for Centres to collaborate and extend their reach including websites, stakeholder support and online collaborative tools.
Strategic Energy Resources Limited's Graphitech (ASX:SER) has entered into an exclusive worldwide licence to commercialise the Intellectual Property generated by the Monash University super capacitor technology project.
Vodafone Australia has formed a strategic partnership with Spotify to broaden its appeal beyond smart phones, inviting customers to take advantage of its 4G network.
An Advanced Manufacturing Business Centre specialising in robots and robotics is being established at Lucas Heights in Sutherland as a result of joint efforts from local business leaders, manufacturers and researchers.
The Northern Territory Department of Education has partnered with NEC Australia to launch iCentre, a portal which aims to simplify problem reporting and improve the department’s visibility and accessibility to support services.
A team of engineering students at the University of New South Wales have broken the record for the fastest electric vehicle over a distance of 500km. SunSwift broke the record of 73km an hour with 106.966km an hour.
Bigtincan hub won the Best Mobile Productivity App at the 2014 Silicon Valley Business App Awards . Xero Touch won the Best Mobile Finance App and the Overall Best App Award.
The Victorian Government has provided a new $6.65 million grant to continue the Victorian Life Sciences Computation Initiative operations at its research hubs presently located at the University of Melbourne (Parkville), Monash University (Clayton) and LaTrobe University (Bundoora). The University of Melbourne has also guaranteed the continued operation of its Centre for Energy-Efficient Telecommunications at Parkville.
Commonwealth Bank has launched an Innovation Lab in Sydney’s CBD which will act as an incubator and accelerator focusing on developing products, services and solutions in collaboration with customers, partners, startups and industry experts.

company watch

Aeeris has lodged a prospectus with ASIC and is seeking to raise $6 million through an initial public offering on the ASX. Aeeris is the developer and operator of the Early Warning Network (EWN), an emergency warning and threat monitoring provider. EWN is a Tech23 Alumni company.
REA Group (ASX:REA) has increased its stake in iProperty Group Limited (ASX:IPP) from 17.2% to 19.4% via the issue of 5 million new shares in iProperty as consideration for the acquisition by iProperty of REA's Hong Kong business, squarefoot.com.hk. iProperty already owns and operates Hong Kong's property portal, Gohome.com.hk, which recently turned profitable.
Tech23 alumni goCatch has raised $6 million in its fourth round of funding attracting investors including James Packer, Seek Co-founder Paul Bassat, and the Liberman and ­Kahlbetzers family.
AdNear , has raised US$19 million from Telstra Ventures and Global Brain with existing investors, Sequoia Capital and Canaan Partners, also participing in the capital raising round. Mark Sherman, Managing Director at Telstra Ventures will now join the AdNear Board of Directors. Founded in 2012, AdNear is now headquartered in Singapore with a presence across Australia, South East Asia, Japan and India.

people watch

Springboard has appointed Liza Noonan as their new General Manager. Noonan was previously at Alcatel Lucent where she led the ng Connect program.
David Yuile has been appointed Managing Director of Nextgen Networks . Yuile is the former CEO of AAPT. Prior to joining AAPT, Yuile was Director of Networks at PowerTel, CEO of 90 East and co-founder of Sales Technology.
