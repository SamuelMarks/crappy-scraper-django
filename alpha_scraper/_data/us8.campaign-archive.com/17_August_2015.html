Atlassian founders Mike Cannon-Brookes and Scott Farquhar are behind a push to ensure "technology" stays in Australian Technology Park. Farquhar and Cannon-Brookes believe that there is no other space which fits so perfectly with what the technology workforce wants - and they have plans to liven the Park area up (think better events/drinking/eating/accommodation options).
