news briefs

Tzukuri won People’s Choice at Tech23 in Sydney last week with 20-year-old founder Allen Liao charming the audience with their unloseable sunglasses innovation. Intelligent Fleet Logistics took away the Innovation Excellence trophy and Clipp won the Greatest Potential Award. Doarama and Bluedot Innovation both won trips to the USA; Bluedot, Clipp, Sound Scouts , Clevertar and Global & Smart all won thousands in cash prizes.
Telstra’s subsidiary Ooyala has acquired Videoplaza. Ooyala will be using the acquisition to tap into the video ad market and build a new business dimension in advertising.
Smiling Mind , the non-profit organisation that has revolutionised meditation for young people through delivering free web and app based programs, has been granted $12 million by the Victorian Government Technology Innovation Fund to run a trial to evaluate the online and application-based education program, evaluating its effectiveness both within classroom environments and as an early intervention tool. The Smiling Mind Education research project is a partnership between Smiling Mind, the Department of Education and Early Childhood Development (DEECD), Insight SRC, Deakin University, and the Young and Well Cooperative Research Centre.
CSIRO has teamed up with St Vincent’s Hospital and Victorian based company Anatomics to implant a titanium-printed heel bone into a 71 year old Victorian man. The titanium was printed using CSIRO’s Arcam 3D printer and implanted with the help of surgeon Professor Peter Choong.
Australia Post has announced 300 regional and rural Post Offices will receive its FlexiPOS point-of-sale technology.
Telstra has launched a new business division, Telstra Health. As part of its launch, Telstra has announced three partnerships. The first partnership with the Northern Territory Government will be to build a new National Telehealth Connection Service to bring specialist medical care to remote communities. The second partnership with Silver Chain Group will be to deliver nursing care directly to a patient’s home via a secure portal for remote monitoring and consultation. Telstra’s final partnership with Swiss based company Medgate provides ‘Telstra ReadyCare’, and aims to allow patients to talk directly to GP’s over video or phone.
Startups in Queensland can tap into $300,000 which the Queensland Government has committed to help Queensland startups gain practical information, advice and networking opportunities. Applications, where collaboration is favoured, will remain open until funds are exhausted or 30th of June 2015.
A new report produced by Vision Critical into The Emerging Collaborative Economy in Australia has revealed an uptake in enthusiasm for collaborative economy platforms that use technology to enable people to connect and share goods and services.

company watch

OneVentures has announced plans to raise $100 million by Christmas or early 2015 after passing the $60 million mark for its Innovation and Growth Fund II. Backers of the fund include Sidney Myer of the Myer Family Company, former Seven Group chief executive officer Peter Gammell, Carlyle Group’s Australian boss Simon Moore and ­Caledonia Investments’ Mark Nelson.
Telstra's muru-D accelerator has invested in its second intake of young companies providing $40,000 seed capital investment and six months of accelerator services in return for 6% equity in the business. The companies are FanFuel, FreightExchange, Wattblock, Disrupt Surfing, You Chews, TripALocal, Peep Digital, VClass, Instrument Works, CrowdSourceHire, and SoccerBrain.
Vocus Communications Limited (ASX:VOC) has announced that it holds an interest of 10% in Amcom Telecommunications Limited (ASX:AMM) with the aim of arriving at an agreement under which Amcom's Board would recommend a combination of the businesses with Amcom's shareholders receiving Vocus shares in lieu of their shares in Amcom.

people watch

Deloitte Australia has appointed Graeme Newton as its new Chief Executive Officer.
Apple Computer Co-founder Steve Wozniak has accepted adjunct professorship at the University of Technology .
Telstra’s new business division, Telstra Health has appointed Shane Solomon as its new Managing Director. Solomon was previously the CEO of the Hong Kong Hospital Authority.
iiNet Founder, Michael Malone is Chairman at Diamond Cyber , a new IT security consulting business.
