news briefs

After achieving successful results in Australia, CSIRO has licensed their Remote-I technology to a Silicon Valley spin-off TeleMedC , who plans to take the technology to the US and world market as part of its 'EyeScan' diagnostic solution. The Remote-I platform works by capturing high-resolution images of a patient's retina with a low-cost retinal camera, which are then uploaded over satellite broadband by a local health worker. Remote-I has been used with over 1000 patients from the Torres Strait Islands and southern Western Australia where 68 patients at risk of going blind were identified, including those with macula odema.
CenITex , the ICT Provider for the Victorian Government is set to survive following the release of the independent review into the Victorian Government’s business support shared services. Reversing the outsourcing of CenITex’s services, two ICT projects, Project Atlas and VicConnect, have been discontinued. CenITex will have a new customer oriented board structure and regular performance monitoring with the implementation of the new governance framework to commence immediately.
Applications for University of Wollongong’s iAccelerate are open until the 28 July 2015, with intake in early September.
1stAvailable (ASX: 1st) and Digital Health Ventures (a company 50% owned by NIB ) have partnered to provide an online booking engine. Under the partnership, 1stAvailable’s online healthcare search and appointment booking service will be used by healthcare providers through Whitecoat.com.au - an online directly of healthcare providers and moderated customer reviews.
iiNet (ASX: IIN) has entered into an agreement with the South Australian Government to deliver its gCloud services to the state’s department of Premier and Cabinet, Planning, Transport and Infrastructure, and Communities and Social Inclusion.
The Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) has selected NASDAQ to deliver its post-trade risk management solution Sentinel Risk Manager.

company watch

My Net Fone Limited (ASX: MNF) has completed a $15 million equity placement, mostly from existing institutional shareholders with a number or new domestic and international shareholders. Settlement is scheduled to occur tomorrow.
Live music performance startup GiggedIn has raised $500,000 in funding led by BlueChilli Venture Fund , Blacksheep Capital , QUT Creative Enterprise Australia , Dominet Digital , Social Family Group and a number of individual angel investors.
M2 Group (ASX: MTU) has completed its acquisition of CallPlus group of companies and related entity, 2Talk Limited, for $246.7 million.
Real estate website OpenAgent has secured $6 million from South African-based Hollard Investments . This is OpenAgent’s second round of funding, which earlier received $2 million from a group of angel investors including Pete Flint, the Founder of US real estate site Trulia .
Newzulu Limited (ASX: NWZ) has entered into a binding agreement to acquire mobile applications and solutions developer Octiplex , with cash and stock acquisition for around $1 million.
Search engine aggregator, Mitula Group (ASX: MUA) has commenced trading on the ASX with a $153.7 million capitalisation following the completion of its IPO, which raised $26.4 million.
Tech23 alumni Affinity Live has closed a $2 million funding round led by Blackbird Ventures and Rotherberg Ventures .
Zoom2u has raised $850,000 to further its expansion throughout Australia. The initial round of funding was co-led by Anthony Klok, a member of Ellerston Capital ’s advisory board, a Temando Non-Executive Director and now member of the Zoom2u board; and Tony Gandel, Founder of Gandel Invest .

people watch

Paul Shetler has been appointed Chief Executive Officer for the federal Digital Transformation Office . Shetler was previously an Executive at the UK’s Government Digital Service and Chief Digital Officer for the UK Ministry of Justice.
The Australian Information Security Association Board of Directors has appointed its first CEO Arno Brok. Brok was previously an Executive with AISA since 2008 and National Director since December 2013.
