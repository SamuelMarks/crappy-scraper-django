NewsBriefs

Australia Post is partnering with the Federal Government to develop solutions for the public to access government digital services . Australia Post will work with the Digital Transformation Agency to integrate its identity technology into the Commonwealth's Digital Identity Framework.
Customers at IKEA Tempe in Sydney can now organise their furniture assembly through a new pilot program with Airtasker .
Vodafone is streets ahead in the latest figures of complaints to the Telecommunications Industry Ombudsman. From January-March 2017, Vodafone numbered 3.9 complaints per 10,000 services in operation, with Telstra and Optus on 9.3 apiece and overall participants sitting on 8.4 per 10,000 services in operation.
A joint electricity purchasing group has been granted permission to form for 11 years. The ACCC authorised the South Australian Chamber of Mines and Energy along with 27 other SA businesses.
CEO of Cicada Innovations Petra Andren has weighed in on the budget, commenting that university cuts combined with 457 visa restrictions "will make it that much harder for Australian deep tech companies to attract and retain talent – the exact companies that could create the industries and jobs of the future if they were given more support to thrive." Cicada Innovations has also recently welcomed Touchphase - a new hardware startup - to its portfolio.
Applications for the University of Wollongong’s iAccelerate program close on 26 June.
EnergyLab 's Acceleration Program is also open for applications. Jobs for NSW provided a $120,000 grant to support the fitout and first year operating costs of EnergyLab.
The Tasmanian Government is providing an assistance package of $1.36 million over five years in a partnership with DXC Technology to create 50 new jobs as the company expands its enterprise delivery centre.
The Federal Government has released a consultation package on reforms to modernise Australia's spectrum management framework. The reforms include new legislation, 'better' integration of management of public sector and broadcasting spectrum, and a review of spectrum pricing.
Tasmania Police will introduce Body Worn Cameras , with the government to spend $3.4 million over four years to roll out the cameras to all frontline police officers. Following a trial, Townsville Hospital is also introducing body-worn cameras on security staff.
Unlockd has signed a deal with UAE telco Etisalat.
The ACT Government is partnering with Canberra company Seeing Machines which will see a $1.35 million trial of self-driving vehicle technology . 40 Canberra drivers will be monitored while driving 'level 3' automated vehicles, which are self-driving most of the time while legally the driver is in control.
The Victorian Government is including $16.4 million in its budget to improve bandwidth and digital connectivity for students in rural and regional Victoria.
Telstra is rolling out 70 4G small cells across Queensland, with the state government approving a further 39 locations .
GetSwift Limited (ASX: GSW) has signed a commercial multi-year agreement with CITO Transport.
Solar power company VivoPower International PLC has announced an alliance agreement with ReNu Energy (ASX: RNE), giving the latter a right of first offer to acquire solar projects originated by VivoPower in Australia below 5MW.
A new Hobart-based research centre will focus on the role of the Southern Hemisphere oceans in the global climate. The Centre for Southern Hemisphere Oceans Research is a collaboration between CSIRO, China's Qingdao National Laboratory for Marine Science and Technology, with support from the University of Tasmania and the University of New South Wales.
Swift Networks Group Limited (ASX: SW1) has won two new contracts, signing a three-year contract with aged care provider Rosewood Care Group in WA to provide its Aged Care Entertainment system to 120 guests at Rosewood's new aged care facility in Perth. Swift will also provide content to Pacific Offshore's Arcadia vessel and its 750 crew members.
QUT is exploring the potential for humanoid robots with the use of 'Pepper', a personal humanoid robot that can recognise emotions. Created by SoftBank Robotics, Pepper will be part of a $1.5 million social robotics research program funded by the Queensland Government's Advance Queensland initiative. The program will involve collaboration with ST Solutions Australia, Softbank Corp's subsidiary, and the Australian Centre for Robotic Vision.
Northrop Grumman Corporation is investing $50 million to develop an advanced defence electronics maintenance and sustainment centre in the Badgerys Creek precinct in Western Sydney. The centre will support advanced electronics including communications equipment, electronic warfare equipment and targeting pods. The company has also opened a repair facility at the Royal Australian Air Force Edinburgh base in South Australia. The facility will provide support services for laser-based Large Aircraft Infrared Countermeasures systems.
What are the challenges for Australia's innovation system ? Innovation and Science Australia want to hear from you - deadline is 31 May.
$7.6 million in Government funding will go towards Square Kilometre Array (SKA) design projects . This is the second round of the SKA Pre-construction Grants Program, and successful applicants include: Curtin University (almost $1.5 million); CSIRO and Aurecon ($1.3 million); Swinburne University of Technology ($444,263); the University of Western Australia and CSIRO ($1.6 million); AARNET and CSIRO ($310,000); and two further CSIRO projects ($1.8 million and $454,800). Research astronomer Dr Lisa Harvey-Smith will be speaking at AgileAus in Sydney next month on her learning journey and measuring black holes!
The final report of the review of ACMA , conducted by the Department of Communications and the Arts, was published today along with the Government's response.
Sendle can now trademark its slogan, 'Post without the office', after IP Australia's decision in favour of Sendle over Australia Post.

PeopleWatch

Dr Tony Worby has been appointed Director of CSIRO's Oceans and Atmosphere Business Unit. Worby was previously CEO of the Antarctic Climate and Ecosystems Cooperative Research Centre.
Clifford Rosenberg is to step down as Managing Director of LinkedIn SouthEast Asia, Australia and New Zealand. Matt Tindale will take on the role.

CompanyWatch

Telstra has invested in California-based cyber security company CrowdStrike, which has just completed a $100 million Series D financing round, led by existing investor Accel. Other existing investor CapitalG and Warburg Pincus participated, with new investors Telstra and March Capital Partners. Telstra is a CrowdStrike customer.
TPG Telecom (ASX: TPM) has completed its retail entitlement offer , raising around $320 million.
