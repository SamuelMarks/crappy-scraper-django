news briefs

REA Group this week announced the creation of REALABS , a purpose-built innovation space to help foster, trial, and bring to life new technologies for consumers to experience property. REA Group says the space “will be dedicated to invention and innovation, housing a range of new technologies, including virtual reality, augmented reality, drones and robotics.” REALABS will also become the home to REAio (formerly Hack Days), a designated space to help develop technology startups and to house industry events.
The Google Impact Challenge Australia has awarded over $5 million to not-for-profit organisations. The five winning organisations - The Nature Conservancy Australia , Great Barrier Reef Foundation , The George Institute for Global Health , Hello Sunday Morning , and Centre for Eye Research Australia all received $750,000. A further five finalists - The Olga Tennison Autism Research Centre , Justice Connect , Australian Marine Environment Protection Association , World Vision Australia , and The Australian Literacy and Numeracy Foundation all received $250,000. The prize grants are to help bring ideas to life to create social change.
As part of the Government's Third Action Plan to tackle domestic violence, $10 million will be used to respond to 'revenge porn' and online abuse. $4.8 million will go towards the development of a national online reporting tool to address the sharing of intimate material without consent. The tool will be managed by the Office of the Children's eSafety Commissioner . Help for domestic violence can be reached through www.1800RESPECT.org.au .
Pacific Petroleum will deploy Promapp's cloud-based business process management software.
Commonwealth Bank and Alipay have signed a Memorandum of Understanding to deliver payment solutions for Australian and Chinese consumers and retailers. The aim of the partnership is to make purchases through Alibaba Group's ecommerce websites easier for Australian consumers.
A Pop Up Innovation Lab was launched in Brisbane last week. Commonwealth Bank's Lab will tour the country, with five stops scheduled for 2017. The Innovation Lab is partnering with the Australian Technology Network of Universities with the aim of strengthening Australia's STEM capacity.
Scamwatch is warning gamblers to avoid sports investment scams during Spring Racing season.
Applications for female entrepreneur accelerator program SheStarts close today.
Queensland Rail is trialling free wifi on its Tilt Train for passengers travelling between Rockhampton, the Wide Bay and Brisbane. And in Tasmania the Hobart Cruise Ship Terminal now also has wifi.
Telco complaints dropped 3.1% over July-September according to the Telecommunications Industry Ombudsman and Communications Alliance . However at 6.2 complaints per 10,000 services in operation, it is over 16% higher than the same period in 2015.
The ACCC has released a discussion paper on whether to declare a wholesale domestic mobile roaming service.
Submissions are invited on the effectiveness of section 593 of the Telecommunications Act 1997, under which the Government provides funding for telecommunications consumer representation and research. ACCAN is currently the sole recipient of funding.
La Trobe University is partnering with Optus in an $8 million strategic alliance . The alliance will initially focus on three objectives: creating a 'Digital University of the Future' through combining a smart cities and 'living lab' approach; embedding data collection and analytics technology in La Trobe's Sport Park development; and developing cyber security courses and employment pathways for graduates. A Chair of Cyber Security will be based at the university.
Hired has chosen Melbourne as their regional headquarters, creating 20 jobs for techies over the next two years.
Neto has partnered with Sendle to deliver a 'frictionless ecommerce experience'. Sendle will be the default delivery provider on the Neto platform.
SpeedCast International Ltd has been chosen as Ericsson's satellite connectivity solution partner enabling Ericsson to expand its solutions for shipping, ports and terminal customers by using SpeedCast's global network and satellite communication services.
Nine Network Australia has contracted Intelsat to distribute programming to the company's six domestic television stations, and provide global satellite and fibre connectivity.
South Australian company WBC Group, in partnership with Flinders University , will use $50,000 in state funding to develop technology to change how power is integrated into the home.
Tomizone Limited (ASX: TOM) has partnered with Hong Kong-based infrastructure solutions provider Macroview Telecom Limited .
Medical technology company Uscom's (ASX: UCM) SpiroSonic digital ultrasonic technologies have been approved for sale in Europe with the receipt of the CE Mark.
iSignthis Ltd (ASX: ISX) will provide IronFX with Paydentity services .
A collaborative research project between Shark Mitigation Systems (ASX: SM8), the NSW Department of Primary Industries , and the University of Technology Sydney will see early warning shark detection system Clever Buoy deployed near Hawks Nest at Port Stephens for a four week period to observe and verify white shark detections.
ABC's Managing Director Michelle Guthrie has spoken about the ABC's digital strategy . She argued that dexterity, diversity and collaboration are needed in 'this fragmented, rapidly evolving media landscape'. The ABC is asking its staff to give news stories an 'equal digital life', and will roll out a series of initiatives and a 'modernised ABC News look' which will be consistent across all platforms and channels. A dedicated Indigenous reporting team will be led by Stan Grant, ABC Editor, Indigenous News Coverage. The organisation is also launching a dedicated news service with third-party provider Chatfuel, also available on Facebook Messenger.

people watch

Airtasker's Co-founder Jonathan Lui is moving to Singapore and leaving his position . Lui will stay on as a Non-Executive Director and advisor.
Matthew Brazier is CyberArk's Regional Director of Australia and New Zealand, and Jeffrey Kok takes on Senior Director of Presales for Asia Pacific and Japan. Brazier was previously at BT Global Services.
The inaugural Women in Research Citation Awards honoured 12 early to mid-career female researchers in Australia last week, in fields ranging from marine ecology to archaeological science.
Vault Intelligence has opened an office in Melbourne, led by former Xero executive Tim Dowling, who was appointed General Manager Commercial Operations in Australia earlier this year.
Tilt Renewables' (ASX: TRE) new CFO and Company Secretary is Steve Symons.
Xero Limited (ASX: XRO) is implementing a flatter organisational structure , with Country Managers to report directly to the CEO. Chief Marketing and Revenue Officer will be replaced by a Chief Marketing Officer, and the company has recently added a Chief People Officer and Chief Accounting Officer. President of Americas at Xero, Russ Fujioka, will depart the company at the end of the year, and will be replaced by Keri Gohman.
Leslie Nassar has passed away in the United States. Nassar was a digital producer at the ABC and was known for building the Q&A system. A fundraising campaign has been set up here .

company watch

Australian B2B telecommunications provider, Fonebox has been acquired by a US NASDAQ listed company for a reported $30 million.
Fintech startup Timelio has raised​ $5 million in seed funding from investors including Thorney Investment Group. The funding comes after Timelio announced growth of 30% per month.
oOh!media Limited (ASX: OML) has completed its acquisition of Executive Channel International Pty Ltd for $68.5 million.
Tech23 alumni Covata Limited (ASX: CVT) is participating in a paid Proof of Concept with three UK government agencies, including the Crown Prosecution Service (CPS), Her Majesty's Revenue and Customers (HMRC), and the National Crime Authority (NCA). The CPS are looking for a solution to broker sensitive digital information related to the prosecution process to and from HMRC, NCA and legal firms.
IRESS (ASX: IRE) has completed its share purchase plan , raising around $15.1 million.
Digital medication management company MedAdvisor Limited (ASX: MDR) has acquired Health Enterprises 2 Pty Ltd for $5.5 million. Healthnotes' founders, Ashley Falting (also Managing Director) and Saurabh Mishra (also Executive Director) will join MedAdvisor's management team.
Prana Biotechnology Limited (ASX: PBT) has received a $4.8 million cash refund under the Government's R&D Tax Incentive Scheme.
NRMA has invested a significant minority share in Divvy Parking , an app that connects commuters with under-used parking spaces.
