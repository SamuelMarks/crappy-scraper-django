company watch

Congratulations to Tech23 Alumnus Agworld for raising a $6million Series C round led by Reed Elsevier Ventures, and with participation from existing investor Yuuwa Capital. This brings Agworld’s total equity raised to $12million. Founded in Perth in 2008 by Doug Fitch and Chris Ramsey, with help soon after from Matthew Powell, Agworld is a provider of information management and cloud collaboration software for farmers and agronomists.
Open Agent has secured $1million from entrepreneurs and investors, including Pete Flint, co-Founder of online real estate company, Trulia (NYSE: TRLA). Zillow recently announced a deal to buy Trulia for $3.5billion.
Led by August Capital, ScriptRock has raised a $9.8million series A round. Participating in the round were Valar Ventures Chairman, Peter Thiel; CEO of Authentic8, Scott Petry and Square Peg Capital. ScriptRock won the Tech23 Greatest Potential Award in 2012.
ilab has entered into an agreement with Artesian Capital to create a $10million investment fund for tech startup companies in Queensland. The fund proposes to invest in over 100 Queensland startups over the course of five years. ilab was established by the Queensland Government in 2000, acquired by The University of Queensland (UQ) in 2009 and then became an independent division of UQ's Uniquest in 2009.
MYOB Finance NZ Limited, a subsidiary of MYOB Holdings Pty Limited, has acquired New Zealand's PayGlobal. PayGlobal was founded in Christchurch in 1991 and provides HR and payroll solutions.
Sam Morgan, a co-founder of TradeMe, has amassed 5% of stock in the Wynyard Group (WYN:NZSX), which specialises in crime analytics, risk and threat assessment software.
Vocus Communications has entered into an agreement to purchase the Bentley Data Centre from ASG Group for $11.7million. The acquisition requires Vocus to supply telecommunications to ASG both locally in Perth and across Australia.
The first investment for Telstra’s Global Applications & Platforms' group has seen Telstra part with $291million to increase ownership in Ooyala from 23% (fully diluted) to 98%. This is in addition to the $65.8million previously invested in Ooyala over the past two years.
SpeedCast (ASX:SDA), a satellite and communications provider, has begun trading on the ASX following its initial public offering.
DDLS , an IT Training Provider, has acquired the Information Technology Infrastructure Library and other intellectual property from ProActive Services following its voluntary liquidation.
Dimension Data has entered into an agreement to purchase Oakton valuing $171million.

news briefs

Australia’s Chief Scientist , Professor Ian Chubb, has said Australia must invest strategically in science to prepare itself for the long haul. “Bluntly, we are middle-of-the-road,” he said while presenting the 2014 Jack Beale Lecture at UNSW. Professor Chubb noted that Australia is the only OECD country not to have a contemporary national science and technology, or innovation strategy. He went on to outline his strategy, which he plans to release on 2nd September in Parliament House.
MyShout has started its collaboration process with Cadbury’s Marvellous Creations. MyShout is part of a group of five Australian entrepreneurs that took part in the Modelez International competition. The other four mobile technology startups selected to help with future in-store engagement with brands such as Cadbury and Philadelphia are: Snaploader , proximiti , skyfii and issue .
Ahmed Fahour, the Managing Director & CEO, of Australia Post delivered a speech last week outlining how digital disruption is affecting Australia Post's business. Posties will deliver 1.2billion fewer letters than they did at the mail peak in 2008. In the last six years, Australia Post has lost 25% of their letter volumes and 24% of customer visits to post offices. The Parcel business has grown from $170million in 2010 to over $300million this year as a result of online shopping.
Vodafone has announced plans to build a call centre in Hobart’s CBD for its staff, employing an additional 240 people during the building phase, due to be completed in December 2014.
Telstra's incubator, muru- D , is accepting applications for its next intake. Information sessions are being held around the country in the next few weeks.
Optus has entered into a two-year engagement to provide ITC Global with expanded satellite capacity.
theSPACE launched Startup Weekend Cairns with Wotif founder Graeme Wood dialing in to officially open the event. The winning team was YakPak, wireless communication for cyclists who ride in packs.
The NSW Government has launched a new website allowing people to provide their feedback and input on the NSW Government’s ICT strategy .

people watch

Vodafone has appointed Anthony Flannery as its new Head of Corporate Media Relations. Flannery was previously at Channel Ten where he was Head of News.
Telstra has appointed Peter Hearl, Chief Operating Officer at Yum, as its new Non-Executive Director. Hearl is also a Non-Executive Director of Goodman Fielder and Treasury Wine Estates in Australia.
ABC Innovation has appointed Louise O’Donnell as its new Head of Strategy. O’Donnell was previously at Network Ten where she was a Product Consultant.
