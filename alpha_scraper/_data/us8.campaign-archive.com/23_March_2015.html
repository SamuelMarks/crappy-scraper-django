Tech23 Alumni InterfereX Communications has raised a further $870k from industry based investors. The InterfereX cloud-based software product, RANifex, addresses the challenges of reducing operational costs of managing wireless infrastructure deployments with indoor and outdoor small cells (access points) for 3G/4G and WiFi. InterfereX is a spinout of NICTA .
