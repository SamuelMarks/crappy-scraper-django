news briefs

Congratulations to Tech23 alumni Bluedot Innovation for receiving $800,000 for their Point Software Development Kit and Etrain Interactive for receiving $398,781 for their cost effective 3D training simulations. They are two of the 17 grant recipients under the Government's latest round of the Entrepreneurs Programme .
The Commonwealth Bank has launched an Innovation Lab in Hong Kong. Building on the interest garnered by their Sydney Innovation Lab, there are plans to launch an additional Innovation Lab in London later this year. The Hong Kong Lab has three main zones - an incubation garage space, collaboration hub and usability room.
Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk has launched Energex ’s Battery Storage Trial which will see the first use of Tesla's PowerWall system in Australia. The trial’s three battery systems will provide Energex with real time data to better understand how best to integrate new technologies into their network. The Queensland Government has a goal of one million rooftops, or 3,000 megawatts of solar PV capacity in Queensland by 2020.
NAB has launched its new mobile payment service NAB Pay, enabling customers to use their mobile phone to make purchases, without the need for a physical card. NAB is the first Australian bank to utilise Visa Token Service in Australia which replaces a customer’s credit card number with a digital ‘token’ that can be used for digital payments, without revealing sensitive account information. Last year, NAB announced a ten-year strategic partnership with Visa to collaborate on payments innovation and product development for customers.
The release of the first national STEM Programme Index (SPI) is now available for school principals. The SPI 2016 guide, which was compiled by the Australian Industry Group and the Office of the Chief Scientist, as part of the STEM Skills Partnerships programme, will have more than 250 science, technology, engineering and mathematics programmes for students.
REFFIND Limited (ASX: RFN) has signed a referral relationship agreement with global provider of Human Capital Management solutions, ADP (NASDAQ: ADP). ADP will provide REFFIND sales referrals and inclusion on the ADP Marketplace and help to accelerate REFFIND's growth in the USA.
Newly listed ASX company Department 13 (ASX: D13) has appointed EPE , as their Australasian provider of Counter Threat EOD and Electronic Countermeausre equipment, as the regional distribution partner in Australia and New Zealand.
Applications for the Advance Queensland Young Starters Program are now open for activities that build entrepreneurial and technology startup skills in young Queenslanders, aged 15-24. The Young Starters Program will open later in 2016.
The NSW Government has released The Collaborative Economy in NSW - position paper outlining a framework to regulate the wider sharing economy in the state, highlighting the “positive impact of the collaborative economy for consumer choice, employment, and productivity.”
Congratulations to healthtech startup CohortIQ for winning a spot inside Pollenizer Incubator for 9-months and $200k seed investment from Right Click Capital and Pollenizer.
Applications for the University of South Australia 's seed funding program Venture Catalyst is now open until Friday 15 April 2016.
A $16.5 million jointly funded project will bring high speed internet to Barcoo and Diamantina in Queensland. The project is funded by the Queensland Government who provided $6.25 million; and Barcoo and Diamantina Shire Councils who contributed over $2 million towards the project.
Microsoft has announced a global alliance with Melbourne IT recruitment startup Gooroo , which exclusively gives Microsoft's Certified Professionals access to Gooroo's career planning tools.

company watch

SEEK Limited (ASX: SEK) has announced that its subsidiary Zhaopin Limited has received an acquisition offer from CDH V Management Company Limited and Shanghai Goliath Investment Management. In October 2006, SEEK acquired an initial 24% interest in Zhaopin Ltd. The Zhaopin investment represented SEEK's first investment in an online employment site outside Australia and New Zealand. Zhaopin listed on the New York Stock Exchange in June 2014 with SEEK retaining an undiluted equity interest of 68.3%.
Applabs Technologies (ASX: ALA) has signed a Big Implementation Agreement to merge with recruitment startup The Search Party (TSP) on the basis of 198 ALA shares for every TSP share. On completion of the transaction, Applabs intends to change its name to Search Party Limited.
Telstra (ASX: TLS) has entered into an agreement to acquire Kloud . Kloud has 150 employees across Melbourne, Sydney, Adelaide and Manila and provides clients with help to move their work to the cloud.
Telstra Ventures has made a strategic investment in Instart Logic . Based in Palo Alto, California. Instart Logic has an application delivery platform combining machine learning and multi-layered security capabilities.
Social entertainment platform MigMe (ASX: MIG) has completed its acquisition of social news site Hipwee in a share deal.
Bitcoin Group Limited is set to list on the Australian Securities Exchange on Tuesday 2 February 2016 under the code BCG.
Melbourne grocery delivery startup YourGrocer has raised $600,000 from Michael Doubinski, investor and adviser of Menulog ; and Envato Founders Colli and Cyan Ta'eed.

people watch

Nic Kennedy is handing over the role of CDO at Vend to Ben Gracewood (the current VP Engineering at Vend) as she takes up the role as Chief Innovations and Ventures Officer at PwC NZ.
Inabox Group (ASX: IAB) has appointed current CEO of subsidiary, Anittel Vincent Pesquet as its new Chief Operating Officer.
The Telecommunications Industry Ombudsman has appointed Geoff Nicholson as its new Independent Director. Nicholson will be replacing Independent Director Jane Harvey in February 2016 who is retiring from the Board.
The Australian Communications and Media Authority is on the search for a Consumer Representative to join it's Consumer Consultative Forum. Applications close Monday 29 February 2016.
