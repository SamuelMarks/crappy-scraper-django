NewsBriefs

Insights into the Australian Open's spectator numbers , movement and dwell times are being tracked through Invigor Group' s (ASX: IVO) Insights Visitor platform, which has been deployed in conjunction with Optus .
Ericsson and Cisco Systems will transform and virtualise Vodafone Hutchison Australia (VHA)'s networks. The transformation program will evolve VHA's core network to increase its agility and programmability from network slicing.
Solar energy will power Melbourne's tram network with a new solar plant to be built in regional Victoria. The Victorian Government will run a tender to build 75MW of large scale solar farms, with 35MW of the new farms to be linked to the tram network.
XTEK Limited (ASX: XTE) will supply an Explosive Ordnance Disposal robot to an unnamed Australian state police service. The ICOR Caliber T5 robot will be used primarily for bomb disposal.
The prime ministers of Japan and Australia have discussed their countries' 'expanding security cooperation' in areas including cyber, space and defence science and technology.
The Australian Privacy Foundation has criticised the Privacy Commissioner v Telstra Corporation Pty Ltd Federal Court judgment that key metadata is not "personal information ". The Foundation has stated that the decision will "severely impair the Privacy Commissioner in regulating privacy online."
The Department of Natural Resources and Mines is to release QImagery , an online digital library of over 800,000 aerial images overlaid on an online map. The collection , some of which dates back to the 1930s, is a reference for topographic mapping and for infrastructure planning such as roads and railway, monitoring coastal landscapes, and for flood management.
Swift Networks Group Limited (ASX: SW1) has won a material contract to supply entertainment content , connectivity and support to the INPEX operated Ichthys LNG Project, located about 220 kilometres off WA.
International driverless car supplier RDM Group is opening its Asia Pacific headquarters in Adelaide. The company plans to develop an assembly facility in the state.
A planned Northern Territory website, MyFuel NT , will give real-time information about fuel prices at service stations across the Territory.
Sydney bus commuters may be able to receive free Wifi with a trial of 50 buses using CATCH technology underway. The trial was developed by APN Outdoor , and if successful will see up to 1000 buses fitted with the technology by the end of the year.
Lahey Constructions will implement the Aconex platform under a three-year enterprise agreement.
Applications are open for the April 2017 auction of spectrum licences in the 700 MHz band. According to ACMA , the band is 'highly valued' for mobile broadband, especially 4G services.
Northern Territory's Power and Water Corporation is using a Tait Communications solution to upgrade its mobile workforce communications system.
The WA Government has awarded ICT infrastructure contracts to Atos , Datacom and NEC . Under the GovNext-ICT Program , agencies only pay for what they use, with all agencies purchasing through one contract where three vendors continuously compete.
South Australian Police will soon use tablet technology to complete up to 95% of duties from within the field. As part of the state’s $7.4 million tablet technology investment, up to 680 vehicles will be fitted with the technology - supplied by Panasonic Toughbooks - with 175 extra tablets for frontline officers.
ikeGPS (ASX: IKE) has signed a multi-territory distribution relationship agreement with Spandex.
If you have a drone, keep it away from bushfires: the Civil Aviation Safety Authority are warning that drones flown near bushfires interfere with aerial firefighting.

PeopleWatch

Co-founder, CEO and Managing Director of carsales.com Limited (ASX: CAR), Greg Roebuck, is to retire . Current COO Cameron McIntyre has been named the next CEO and MD.
Craig Smith has joined the Agile Alliance Board of Directors . Smith is an Agile coach and Director of Unbound DNA. He has been a speaker and advisor at the Agile Australia Conference , and this year is a Stream Captain.
Alex Stokes is to join Envato as Programme Manager. Prior to this she was Engineering Manager at REA Group. Alex Stokes is a Stream Captain for AgileAus this year.
Springboard Enterprises Australia has welcomed Julie Demsey as General Manager. Demsey has moved from Silicon Valley where she held an Enterprise Product role with Rally Health.
Arthur Sinodinos is to become the Minister for Industry, Innovation and Science .
Digital classifieds group company The Mitula Group Limited (ASX: MUA) has appointed Georg Chmiel to its Board of Directors. Chmiel was previously CEO and Managing Director of iProperty Group.
Chief Technology Officer for TIBCO Software Asia Pacific and Japan is Robert Merlicek. Merlicek was most recently the company's Director of Strategic Solutions.
Ben Malone is LiveHire 's (ASX: LVH) new Chief Financial Officer. Malone was previously at REA Group and Australian Unity.
Continuum have opened a regional headquarters in Sydney, with Frank Bauer to lead as new APAC Managing Director.
Vodafone has appointed its Director of Sales, Ben McIntosh, to the newly created role of Consumer Business Unit Director.
Robotic process automation software company Blue Prism has opened a new Sydney office and appointed Mike Cawsey as Regional Director of Sales for ANZ.

CompanyWatch

Activistic (ASX: ACU) has become the registered holder of 95.1% of Plus Connect's fully paid ordinary shares. Paul Crossin has been named Executive Director of Activistic. The takeover will enable Activistic to expand its fundraising platforms for charities.
