NewsBriefs

Satellite communications company and Tech23 alumnus Myriota will invest $2.72 million in an Internet of Things laboratory in its hometown of Adelaide. Myriota’s investment of $1.36 million will be matched by a grant from the South Australian State Government’s Future Jobs Fund.
Webjet has expanded its Blockchain initiative RezChain to signup external partners including Thomas Cook, Dida Travel and Mitra Global.
Brisbane-based cyber security startup Entersoft has been awarded 2017 FinTech Startup of the Year at the Hong Kong FinTech Awards.
Victorian food and AgTech incubator program Rocket Seeder has partnered with MLA Donor Company, a subsidiary of Meat & Livestock Australia, to explore opportunities for innovation with red meat .
A new industry initiative to promote AgTech and entrepreneurship , the Asia Pacific AgTech Alliance , has been founded by SproutX, a Melbourne-based AgTech startup accelerator led by Andrew Lai, among others.
The Sydney Startup Hub , a $35 million, 11 floor facility at Wynyard Station has been officially opened . It will be one of the eight locations in the world to host Microsoft’s ScaleUp program.
Telstra’s muru-D has announced its fifth cohort, ushering 10 new startups into the accelerator program. University of Sydney’s INCUBATE has likewise welcomed nine new startups to their cohort.
Caltex (ASX:CTX) has joined forces with Slingshot to launch a new accelerator .
CSIRO scientists have developed new technology that supercharges water purification and has the ability to make Sydney Harbour water drinkable. Using their own specially designed form of graphene, 'Graphair', CSIRO researchers were able to create a film with microscopic nano-channels that let water pass through, but stop pollutants.
Axon Public Safety Australia has confirmed the sale of 11,000 Body cameras to Victoria Police.
Arc , a dedicated hub for hardware-focused startups, is set to be opened in Brisbane with help from $270,000 in funding from the Federal Government’s Incubator Support program.
ANSTO is using nuclear techniques to bring new insights into how structure contributes to function in food.
A driverless shuttle will be trialled on the beachfront at Glenelg, shuttling passengers between smart shuttle stops at Moseley Square and the Broadway Kiosk. South Australian company SAGE Automation has joined forces with US vehicle manufacturer Local Motors to run the trial.
Canberra-based cyber security provider Penten has been awarded a $1.3 million innovation contract to develop secure wireless devices to protect classified information used by the Australian Army.
Momentum Cloud has received an Accelerating Growth Loan from Jobs for NSW to build its marketing and sales capacity.

CompanyWatch

Sex industry blockchain startup intimate has raised $4.5 million in pre-sale ahead of full ICO next month.
Cloud services provider Bulletproof 's (ASX:BPF) independent review board has recommended that shareholders support a $24.7 million bid for the company from AC3, a privately owned ICT managed services firm.
Software company LiveTiles (ASX:LVT) has raised $20 million through a share placement.
Blockchain startup Havven is eyeing $30 million in pledged funds for its initial coin offering as it moves towards creating one of the first successful ‘stablecoins’.

PeopleWatch

Sarah-Belle Murphy, former Executive Manager of Media Product at REA (ASX:REA), will return to Bauer Media as General Manager of Digital .
Matt Berriman is stepping down as CEO of Unlockd due to a battle with mental illness. Jane Martino will be stepping up as CEO in his place.
University of Queensland has appointed Ben Coughlin as Chief Student Entrepreneur .
Deputy Chief Executive of CSIRO Craig Roy is leaving his role after 15 years . CSIRO's Data61 CEO Adrian Turner will step up as an ex-officio member to the Executive Team.
