Telstra (ASX: TLS) and NEP Australia have successfully trialled remote broadcast television production across the Pacific Ocean from Sydney to Los Angeles.
In partnership with the Great Barrier Reef foundation , a $444 million agreement with the Federal Government will seek to tackle coral-eating starfish, reduce pollution and mitigate impacts of climate change. Initiatives will involve the adoption of new technologies to help improve water quality and enhance Reef health monitoring.
NBN Co has announced new R&D relationships with the University of Melbourne and the University of Technology Sydney. This collaboration will focus on the R&D of technologies such as robotics, Internet of Things, data analytics, artificial intelligence and smart cities.
Big congratulations to Petra Andren and her team at Cicada Innovations for winning 2018 Global Incubator of the Year from The InBIA: International Business Innovation Association. Cicada is a long-time supporter of Tech23 .
Motorola Solutions has signed a $261 million contract to upgrade the Metropolitan Mobile Radio network for Victoria Police, Ambulance Victoria and the Metropolitan Fire Brigade to enhance the greater metropolitan voice communication for emergency services.
Phase two of the Murchison Widefield Array low frequency radio telescope development is now underway . By doubling the number of antennas at the Murchison Widefield Array in remote Western Australia, the upgraded radio telescope will become 10 times more powerful.
The Department of Defence will invest $5 million over the next five years to develop fatigue testing technologies for military helicopter pilots in collaboration with industry and the US Navy. Scientists will use a retired US Navy Seahawk to develop the technologies.
The Queensland government is inviting app developers to submit expressions of interest by 4th May to create a new commercial fishing app.
Canberra drivers are invited to take part in automated vehicle trial as part of the $1.5 million ACT government industry partnership with local company Seeing Machines . The trial will test how quickly a driver can be ready to resume control of an automated vehicle.
The new University of Western Australia's Wave Energy Research Centre will be based in the former Albany Visitor Centre. $3.75 million in state government funding will see UWA establish the centre as a hub for wave energy expertise, research and development.
A research trial between QUT , Agriculture Victoria and the Plant Biosecurity CRC will explore how drones can act as weapons against tiny pests that are plaguing the grape industry.
The Federal Government will assign $70 million to replace tech infrastructure at the Pawsey Supercomputing Centre in Perth.
Blockchain company Power Ledger has landed a trial partnership to roll out its peer-to-peer energy trading with Japan’s second-largest electricity company Kansai Electric Power Co (KEPCO).
ASX has released a consultation paper outlining the planned new features and timetable for replacing CHESS with a distributed ledger technology solution. Feedback is being sought on the timing, testing and transition arrangements for the new system, and on the new business requirements captured in the plan. The new system is estimated to go live between the fourth quarter of 2020 and the first quarter of 2021 (calendar years).
COMPANY WATCH
Canva has announced its acquisition of interactive presentation startup Zeetings.
Australian-founded, Texas-based ecommerce company BigCommerce has raised $85 million in a funding round led by Goldman Sachs, with participation from existing backers General Catalyst, GGV Capital, and Tenaya Capital. With this new funding, BigCommerce has now raised over US$200 million.
Congratulations to Melbourne-founded location services company Bluedot Innovation who have raised $7.24 million in a Series A round led by Transurban, with participation from investors including Jeff Katz and founding CFO of PayPal, David Jacques. Bluedot’s Co-founder Filip Eldic presented at Tech23 in 2014.
Snappr has acquired competitor and fellow photography business Photographers.com.au.
Media startup Oovvuu will receive $3.8 million in a raise led by CapitalPitch and Cygnet Capital.
Redflow Limited (ASX: RFX) will raise $18.1 million from a combination of a placement to new investors and a fully underwritten rights issue offered to all current shareholders.
Rent (ASX: RNT), the company behind rental listings platform Rent.com.au, will acquire RentPay, a payments system for the rental market, from MYOB (ASX: MYO) for $425,000.
First Growth Funds Limited (ASX:FGF) has announced it has reached an agreement with Heuresy LLC for a $2.98 million investment in CryptoData Vault LLC .
PEOPLE WATCH
Alcidion (ASX: ALC) has spent $12 million acquiring MKM Health and its affiliate, Patientrack Holdings. As part of the transaction CEO of MKM Health, Kate Quirke will be appointed to the role of Chief Executive of the enlarged Alcidion Group and will join the Alcidion Board as an Executive Director. Ray Blight, Alcidion’s Chairman and CEO, will move to the role of Executive Chairman.
Joel MacDonald has stepped down as CEO of logistics software company GetSwift (ASX: GSW) and is to be replaced by Bane Hunter.
