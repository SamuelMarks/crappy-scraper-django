NewsBriefs

The MinEx CRC (Cooperative Research Centre) is to receive $50 million from the Federal Government. Building on the collaborations of the DET CRC, MinEx CRC will pioneer cheap, safe and environmentally friendly coiled tubing drilling to build a 3D picture of the subsurface.
Green Sync has received $7.7 million from the Australian Renewable Energy Agency to fund Simply Energy’s Virtual Power Plant in South Australia, which will deploy Green Sync’s deX (decentralised energy exchange) technology for the first time. The $23 million project will deliver Tesla Powerwall 2 home batteries to up to 1200 Adelaide households.
Telstra (ASX: TLS) has turned on its world-first precinct of 5G-enabled Wi-Fi hotspots at the Gold Coast for public testing, as well as Australia’s first 5G-connected car.
Charter Hall (ASX: CHC) has partnered with Melbourne-based Collective Campus to launch a five-month proptech accelerator which will invest in and partner with InSpaceXR, BricksandAgent.com, Snaploader, and Estate baron.
Vodafone is to enter the streaming TV market with a new Netflix partnership and Vodafone TV - its first dedicated streaming media device.
Macquarie Telecom (ASX: MAQ) will deploy software-defined wide area networking (SD-WAN) services for Hall & Prior Aged Care across Western Australia and New South Wales providing new audio and video conferencing solutions to aged care residents.
Power Ledger has unveiled the first commercial deployment of their energy trading platform in Melbourne through their partnership with Greenwood Solutions.
Vertiv has partnered with Ericsson Energy Alliance in a push for next-gen telecom access networks.
Transurban (ASX: TCL) has partnered with Transport for NSW, Roads and Maritime services to initiate NSW’s largest on-road automated vehicle technology trial to collect data for future enhancements of road infrastructure. And in Victoria it is not too late to nominate to ride on the Autonobus being trialled near La Trobe University! The autonomous bus is a collaboration between VicRoads, Keolis Downer, La Trobe University, HMI, RACV and ARRB.
Simble Solutions Limited (ASX: SIS) has announced an exclusive partnership to be the only energy management solution to be marketed and promoted by Optus.
Any Australian technology start-ups and not-for-profit members of the ecosystem who provide solutions for disadvantaged Australians should consider applying for funding from Optus Future Makers program.
Provisional drivers aged 17-24 in Western Sydney or regional NSW are being invited to trial telematics technology, which is like a ‘black box’ for cars. The trial hopes to help drivers become safer. Participants will be paid $100.
The NSW Law Reform Commission is welcoming input on whether legislation is needed to regulate who can access the digital assets (photos, videos, emails, online banking accounts) of a person who has died.

CompanyWatch

Blackbird ’s third venture fund of $225 million has been announced. Led by Hostplus, investors include First State Super, the Future Fund, clients of Cambridge Associates and 100 private individuals.
Xinja , which is working to become Australia’s first digital bank has finalised an equity crowdfunding raising of $2.16 million from investors. Xinja has also announced its response to the Productivity Commission’s draft report into competition in the Australian Financial.
The Australian Competition and Consumer Commission has concerns about MYOB ’s (ASX: MYO) proposed acquisition of Reckon’s Accountants Group (ASX: RKN), saying it would likely be the only supplier of software suitable for medium to large accounting firms. Submissions are being accepted until 13 April.
Crop Logic (ASX: CLI) has announced the strategic acquisition of AG Logic Pty Ltd due to Tasmania being a key potato growing state and Ag Logic having a good reputation. CropLogic builds upon 30 years of scientific research by The New Zealand Institute of Plant and Food Research and has an analytical platform to optimise field productivity. AG Logic’s agriculture mapping tools are to be acquired for up to $320k in a cash/stock split.
Blackbird Ventures and Telstra Ventures have invested $4.8 million in Brisbane based MOVUS which spun out of University of Queensland’s iLabs and won the Collaboration Award at Tech23 in 2015.
Seven West Media ’s (ASX: SWM) 50% stake in Yahoo7 will be acquired by US JV partner Oath.
Congratulations to the team at Myriota ! Myriota has been backed by Main Sequence Ventures and Blue Sky Venture Capital, Boeing HorizonX Ventures, Singtel Innov8 and Right Click Capital in a $19.4 million series A funding round . Myriota was spun out of the University of South Australia in 2015 and showed off their direct to satellite communications platform at Tech23 in 2016.
AI startup Daisee , run by Google Australia’s former Managing Director Richard Kimber, has raised $8.8 million in funding .
Tempus Partners has raised a second fund, Tempus Fund 2, a $40M+ Early Stage Venture Capital Limited Partnership to invest in Seed and Series A stages. Tempus' first fund invested in the likes of Freelancer and Clipp.
HappyCo has completed the third and final tranche of its Series A funding round led by Sydney-based Alium Capital Management. The company was founded in Adelaide in 2011, was one of the Tech23 in 2012 and moved its headquarters soon after to San Francisco. The total value of the entire Series A round is USD $10.8MM.

PeopleWatch

Creina Chapman has been appointed as Deputy Chair and CEO of the Australian Communications and Media Authority . She is currently the Head of Regulatory Affairs and Corporate Communications at Southern Cross Austereo. Chris Jose and Fiona Cameron have been appointed as new full-time members and James Cameron has been re-appointed as a full-time member.
David Whiteing, Group Executive Enterprise Services and Chief Information Officer, will leave the Commonwealth Bank in the next couple of months. The changes in leadership coincide with Matt Comyn taking on the role of CEO . Paul Newham, currently Executive General Manager Group Operations, will take acting responsibilities for the Enterprise Services division while a global search is undertaken for Whiteing’s successor.
Polymath co-founder and Managing Director Jimmy Van has joined GLiNTECH as part of their acquisition of Atlassian’s new app, Dash.
UK-based adtech startup illuma has officially launched in Australia and New Zealand, appointing Lance Traore as Country Manager .
Redflow (ASX: RFX) has appointed Tim Harris as its new CEO . Harris was previously Chief Commercial Officer for Chorus.
Cancer Researcher Dr Misty Jenkins has won the STEM Professional Career Achievement Award at the second Indigenous STEM Awards , funded by CSIRO and BHP Billiton Foundation.
