NewsBriefs

12 digital, biological, chemical, botanical and fingerprint analysts will work at the new Victorian Police forensics hub in the Latrobe Valley. The team will target the spread of ice and other drugs.
QBE Insurance (ASX: QBE) is using drones to help process claims in its European and North American operations. Following the Ecuadorean earthquake, QBE used drone technology to handle claims. 90% of large claims were resolved within 90 days of the event.
Optus is to begin rolling out 4.5G services in selected capital cities over the next 12 months with the help of Huawei.
Wesley Mission Queensland has rolled out SugarCRM software in partnership with Loaded Technologies .
Spark NZ has partnered with Netflix - the first time Netflix has been bundled with broadband in New Zealand.
The new Internet of Things Security Guideline has been launched by IoT Alliance Australia.
In the wake of Israel's prime minister visit, Australia and Israel have signed an Agreement on Bilateral Cooperation in Technological Innovation and Research and Development.
And the knowledge-sharing program between Israel and the NSW Government will continue in 2017. A second delegation of NSW startups will be sent to Tel Aviv for mentoring.
Swinburne University has launched the Data Science Research Institute to create new data technologies to support researchers working across diverse fields, from biology to economics.
ASI Solutions has deployed Meru Networks-based wifi infrastructure at Marion City Council.
Telstra has selected Ericsson to support programs within Telstra's 'Network of the Future' transformation program . The program includes a national optical network transformation and expansion; 5G New Radio trials; a media delivery cloud; and the deployment of what Telstra calls Australia's "largest Internet of Things (IoT) network" via the deployment of Cat M1 functionality.
She Flies has received just under $10,000 in state government funding to conduct drone lessons for Queensland schoolgirls .
QPC is rolling out a telephony and workforce management system for Service SA, based on Genysys PureConnect.
SA Health has extended its use of Objective (ASX: OCL) across the Department for Health and Ageing, selecting ECM 10, Objective Perform and Objective Ministerial Correspondence.
The Federal Government has launched its first Joint Cyber Security Centre in Brisbane. It marks the first stage of a $47 million program which pilots collaborative work spaces for industry, government and law enforcement. The Brisbane centre is led by CERT Australia.
Expressions of interest close on 3 April 2017 for the Victorian Government’s $2 million Waste to Energy Infrastructure Fund . A full application and detailed business case assessment process will follow for eligible project ideas.
Quality Connex will provide a cloud contact centre solution to MyRepublic under a five-year agreement.
The Digital Transformation Agency has rolled out its expanded Digital Marketplace with new areas of digital expertise including cyber security, data science, content and publishing, marketing, communications and engagement.
The National Film and Sound Archive of Australia built a custom-made device, combined with advanced compositing software, to digitise and publish a rare film reel from 1901 featuring cricketers K.S. Ranjitsinhji and C.B. Fry.
Cognizant has opened a Digital Business Collaboratory in Melbourne.
FOX SPORTS is extending its relationship with NEP Australia through a new six-year deal which includes two new NEP-developed hubs in Sydney and Melbourne that enable multiple, concurrent outside broadcasts.
Netherlands-based RoutIT will deploy Dubber's (ASX: DUB) Cloud Call recording platform.

CompanyWatch

DWS Limited will acquire SMS Management & Technology . Under a Scheme of Arrangement , SMS shareholders will receive $1.00 cash and 0.39 DWS shares for every SMS share (an implied value of $1.66 per share).
Fairfax Media Limited is to conduct a strategic review of the Domain Group in preparation of it potentially listing as a separate entity by the end of 2017. The separation would result in Fairfax continuing to own a controlling (60%-70%) share with Domain Group CEO Antony Catalano remaining at the helm.
Online small business lender Prospa has secured $25 million in growth funding - led by AirTree Ventures .
Afterpay (ASX: AFY) and Touchcorp Limited (ASX: TCH) have signed a heads of agreement for a proposed merger. The in-principle agreement would see the companies merge under a newly incorporated Australian holding company, NewCo.
After last week announcing its acquisition of Hostworks, Inabox (ASX: IAB) has completed a $4 million capital raise via a private placement.
Telstra has sold its remaining 6.5% interest in Chinese online business Autohome to Ping An Insurance for $282 million.
The New Zealand Commerce Commission has declined the proposed merger between Vodafone New Zealand and SKY Network Television (ASX: SKT).

PeopleWatch

Gavin Gibson has joined the Board of .au Domain Administration Ltd (auDA). Gibson is COO and Executive of Dreamscape Networks and Crazy Domains. Gibson replaces Kartic Srinivasan who has moved out of the industry.
