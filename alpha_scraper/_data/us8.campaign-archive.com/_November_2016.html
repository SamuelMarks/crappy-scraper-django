news briefs

UNSW and Tata Consultancy Services are partnering to pursue technology research collaborations . The organisations will work on machine learning, virtual reality, robotics, data analytics and cloud computing.
The NSW Government has announced a $500 million funding package to support a range of innovative environmental programs to tackle climate change. Have your say on the plans here .
Australia's data policy framework needs a major overhaul, according to The Productivity Commission . The Commission suggests the introduction of a 'Comprehensive Right', giving people more control over their data.
SlatteryIT has adopted Joviam as their cloud provider - a hyper converged architecture based on a customised Xen hypervisor, backed by InfiniBand supercomputer technology.
From December 2016, Sydney train customers will be able to receive personalised messages through Twitter if their journey is disrupted. The three month pilot will be launched by Transport for NSW in partnership with Twitter.
Expressions of interest from industry and tech leaders are currently being accepted by NSW Transport to run a trial of on-demand transport services .
The NSW Government and AGL will invest $500,000 each in a pilot program to install solar panels on 250 properties in low-income households.
Vodafone Hutchison Australia (VHA) and Nokia have partnered to deliver a proof of concept demonstrating how Mobile Edge Computing (MEC) can improve public safety by processing data feeds from sensors like CCTV cameras in real-time, tracking people in defined perimeter zones, and distinguishing the movement of people and vehicles. VHA has signed a Memorandum of Understanding to join Nokia's Mission Critical Communications Alliance.
The public are invited to get involved in the second Great Koala Count planned in South Australia on 26-27 November by downloading the locally developed phone app, Koala Counter, and taking a walk to document the koalas they see.
As part of its new innovation strategy , the Western Australian Government will launch a $20 million innovation package to 'increase the number of local innovations that are successfully commercialised, invest in local talent, attract the best and brightest minds to work in WA, and encourage investment in local innovators from across the globe.'
Low-income consumers may struggle to stay connected to telecommunications services, according to a new report released by ACCAN and the South Australian Council of Social Service . The report found that while 66% of respondents consider telecommunications in their top five household budget items, 62% have difficulty paying, cut back or stopped using services. The report calls for reform to the Centrelink Telephone Allowance.
Amazon Web Services Direct Connect is now hosted at NEXTDC 's M1 Melbourne data centre, making it the third point of presence in Australia and the first outside Sydney.
Health-tech accelerator HCF Catalyst is open for applications. The program was developed by HCF and Slingshot . Applications close on 25 November.
PowerHealth Solutions has partnered with Adaptive Insights to combine the latter's modelling and planning with the former's costing and billing products.
The ACCC has released the third quarterly NBN Wholesale Market Indicators Report . According to the report, nbn is supplying 252,319 more broadband wholesale access services than the last reporting period. Telstra unsurprisingly is the largest acquirer of wholesale access services nationwide, with a wholesale market share of 49.7%.
Optus has launched its Advanced Security Operations Centre , powered by Trustwave .
HCF is partnering with digital health platform Healthshare . GPs will be able to search and select specialists participating in the HCF Known Gap and No Gap scheme.
Freeview has launched Freeview FV , a mobile app enabling live streaming of 16 TV channels and catch-up content from free-to-air networks.
Crowd-sourced technology and media company Newzulu Limited (ASX: NWZ) has signed a technology licensing and content agreement with West Australian Newspapers Limited, part of Seven West Media Limited . The six-figure agreement will see Seven West Media license the full suite of Newzulu's technology products for its newspaper division and digital assets.
The results are in for Fintech Australia 's EY FinTech Australia Census for 2016. Only 13% of fintech leaders are female, with an average number of 22% females in the workplace. 76% of capital came from private funding, and 54% of fintech companies are based in NSW, followed by 28% in Victoria.
All South Australian primary schools will have at least one STEM specialist teacher by 2019. A new "STEM Play" program will also be implemented in all Department of Education and Child Development preschools.
BSA Limited (ASX: BSA) has signed a three plus three year contract with Foxtel . The new contract continues the company’s strategic partnership arrangement.

company watch

Tech23 alumni Fillr (previously Pop Tech), has raised $5 million from Softbank China Capital , Reinventure and Southern Cross Venture Partners to help users complete online forms.
Vodafone has acquired Lebara's mobile customer base and business assets in Australia.
Global satellite communications and network services provider SpeedCast (ASX: SDA) will acquire Harris CapRock for approximately $560 million . Harris CapRock provides communications networks for remote and harsh environments.
Carnegie Wave Energy (ASX: CWE) has received a $15.5 million European Regional Development Fund grant to support the first phase of its 15MW commercial wave energy project at Wave Hub, Cornwall, in the UK.
The ACCC will not oppose Tabcorp Holdings Limited's (ASX: TAH) proposed acquisition of gaming and promotional management systems provider Intecq Limited (ASX: ITQ).

people watch

The new chair of The Australian Information Industry Association (AIIA) is John Paitaridis, Managing Director of Optus Business. Kate Burleigh (Managing Director, Intel Australia), Marie Johnson (Managing Director and Chief Digital Officer, Centre for Digital Business), and Kee Wong (Managing Director, e-Centric Innovations) were all re-elected. A further three positions will be filled in the coming months.
Incoming ANZ Group Executive, Technology at ANZ Bank, Gerard Florian , has been at Dimension Data for over 15 years.
CSC has announced a new regional Chief Technology Officer group, led by Sam Johnston and comprising seven 'subject matter experts'. The company also launched an Innovation Centre at Macquarie Park, Sydney.
Verizon Enterprise Solutions has appointed Robert Le Busque as Managing Director of Australia, New Zealand and India, and David Kim as Regional Managing Director for Asia and Japan.
Peter Tazewell is DTI Group Ltd 's (ASX: DTI) new Chief Executive Officer . Tazewell was previously CFO of Matrix Composite & Engineering and CEO of Sungrid.
