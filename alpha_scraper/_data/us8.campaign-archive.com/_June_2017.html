NewsBriefs

The new 2017 Cadillac CTS sedan contains Cohda Wireless' Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) software. Cohda is supplying their Dedicated Short Range Communications solutions for the 2017 Cadillac CTS, sold in the US and Canada.
CSIRO has released its Low Emissions Technology Roadmap . It includes four options for decarbonisation of the energy sector - which currently accounts for 79% of Australia's emissions.
Migrants and refugees in Victoria are encouraged to get involved in the state's startup sector, with LaunchVic's third round of funding to invest between $50,000-$500,000 per application for organisations to deliver educational, acceleration, incubation, mentoring or other programs for Victorian migrants and refugees.
Optus - who have provided the Department of Defence with the satellite communication services through the Optus C1 satellite and on-board Defence Payload System since 2003 - will reconfigure the satellite to reduce on-board fuel usage and extend its life as far as 2027. The current agreement is due to expire in 2020, with the cost of the contract sitting at around $40 million over the next 10 years.
Vodafone Hutchison Australia (VHA) has filed judicial review proceedings following the ACCC's draft decision on domestic roaming. VHA is asking the Federal Court to review the ACCC's inquiry process on behalf of Australian mobile customers citing the process is too vague.
Mobilicom has partnered with Next Vision to provide commercial drone manufacturers with secure bi-directional communication with a dual-channel stabilised camera.
Family Zone Cyber Safety (ASX: FZO) has partnered with Edunet Computer Services to enable Edunet to pre-install Family Zone software on devices sold to parents under school mandates.
LiveHire Limited (ASX: LVH) has signed and launched regional health services provider Barwon Health's Live Talent Community .
Queensland’s Redland City Council is moving to a modular data centre from Vertiv .
A Queensland coal seam gas site is the location of Imagine Intelligent Materials' second commercial installation of its imgne X3 solution.
A new algorithm could lead to the early detection of cancer. CSIRO's Data61 is developing a software tool to improve the detection of angiogenesis - the development of new blood vessels, which is known to precede the growth of cancers.

NEC has opened its $4.38 million Global Security Intelligence Centre in Adelaide. The company is also developing facial recognition software for SA Police.
Following an investigation into whether Optus was providing less data than advertised to its customers, the ACCC has accepted a court enforceable undertaking from Optus, agreeing to compensate affected customers.
Technology which triggers safety measures when vehicles are detected approaching intersections is to be installed at four rural locations in South Australia. The Rural Intersection Active Warning System can reduce the speed limit when it detects vehicles approaching an intersection.
NEXTDC 's C1 Canberra data centre will host Amazon Web Services Direct Connect.
A new mySA GOV app allows South Australians to store government-issued pass and licence info on their smartphones and devices.
mHITS is partnering with Easypaisa , Telenor Microfinance Bank's mobile money platform, to enable money transfers from Australia to Pakistan.
The Chelsea Football Club and the Chelsea Foundation will use Xref Limited 's (ASX: XF1) candidate referencing services.
RES Australia and Telstra have signed a long-term corporate power purchase agreement to support the development of a 70 MW solar farm in Queensland. Expected to be operational by the latter half of 2018, the farm will generate enough renewable energy to power up to 35,000 homes.
BAE Systems Australia will upgrade the Royal Australian Navy's maritime satellite communications terminals in a four year, $30 million project. The upgrade of MASTIS (Maritime element of the Advanced SATCOM Terrestrial Infrastructure System) will create 20 new jobs and is an extension of the company's existing MASTIS support contract.
Medical Developments International Limited (ASX: MVP) has signed an agreement with CSIRO to develop new manufacturing technologies for pharmaceutical products. MVP will invest $3 million in the project over three years.
The Gold Coast's broadband speeds are set to improve with high speed fibre optic cabling being laid to boost CCTV capacity and support the city's Disaster Management Unit operations. The fibre optic cable is supplied by AFL and installed by Universal Communications Group.

CompanyWatch

SaaS platform PASCAL51 has raised $250,000 in seed capital.
NAB’s venture capital fund, NAB Ventures, has led a $32 million Series D funding round in Toronto-based cloud fintech company, Wave . NAB Ventures were joined by other investors including Royal Bank of Canada, Silicon Valley venture firms CRV and Social Capital, global funds OurCrowd and Harbourvest. NAB Ventures has the right to appoint an observer to the Wave board.
Square Peg Capital has invested $4 million in event driven email platform Vero . Both founders hatched the idea of Vero through their experience as part of the Startmate accelerator - it's worth reading about it here .
Gilmour Space Technologies has raised $5 million in Series A funding, led by Blackbird Ventures. Other investors include 500 Startups. The company expects to launch its first commercial rockets to suborbital space by the end of 2018.
Frontier Digital Ventures Limited (ASX: FDV) has invested in two southeast Asian online classified portals . The company has invested $3.1 million in AutoDeal and $1.6 million in Propzy .
Telstra has acquired UK-based technology services company Company85 .
