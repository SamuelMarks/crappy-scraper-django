news briefs

Tonight's Four Corners (ABC, 8.30pm) will air their highly-anticipated report on ' Cyber War : how hackers are threatening everything from your bank account to the nation's secrets'.
Australian tech startups are encouraged to apply to be included on Startrail , the Australian startup ecosystem map.
Today marked the beta launch of The Digital Transformation Office (DTO)'s Digital Marketplace . Assistant Minister for Cities and Digital Transformation, The Hon Angus Taylor MP, was on hand for the launch.
Optus will introduce ' small cells ' mobile coverage through Optus satellites from Katherine to Uluru along the Stuart Highway.
The STEM.I.AM program led by indigenous creative agency Carbon Media, with Advance Queensland on-board as the foundation sponsor will encourage young Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders to participate in science, technology, engineering and maths at university.
Both Victoria and NSW are handing out money for startups. Through the Jobs for NSW Fund , startups will be assisted with $10 million to grow NSW's network of incubators and accelerators, and $3 million over 2016-17 for direct grants.
LaunchVic has provided $6.5 million in funding for 18 projects include Startupbootcamp; Social Traders; the Lemonade Stand startup program for kids; a university accelerator program led by La Trobe University in partnership with Deakin and Federation universities; and a startup hub based in Geelong. Applications for the second round of funding will open next month.
ANU researchers will study the progression of Parkinson’s disease by collecting data through brain imaging, eye tracking, visual perception and postural sway, and then collaborating with researchers from the ANU College of Engineering and Computer Science to analyse the data. The study, based out of Canberra Hospital, will involve 120 people with Parkinson's (as well as a control group without Parkinson's). The five-year research program will commence next month.
The world's second fastest supercomputer (based in China), was used to run part of the software system which will manage data from the Square Kilometre Array. The prototype science data processor execution framework was deployed on the Tianhe-2 supercomputer by an international team led by Professor Tao An from Shanghai Astronomical Observatory in China and Professor Wicenec in Western Australia.
Evergen is using CSIRO-developed technology in its intelligent home energy management system, which makes ‘smart decisions’ based on household power consumption patterns and local weather.
Imagine IM has completed the first independent field trials of its imgne X3 coating for geotextiles.
nbn released its plans last week for the complete network rollout through to 2020 in its 2017 Corporate Plan .
REA Group has selected MuleSoft's Anypoint Platform to support the company's ongoing growth.
The Australian Federal Police executed a search warrant at Parliament House last Wednesday in relation to the alleged unauthorised disclosure of Commonwealth information relating to the nbn co.
Deakin University has selected the AppSense Environment Manager to improve users’ logon experience. After its initial deployment to 300 machines, AppSense will be implemented across 10,000 machines.
Adelaide City Council is using Skype for Business's module Skype Meeting Broadcast to live stream its Council Committee Meetings.
The Brisbane Lions Australian Football Club has chosen ShoreTel Connect to provide a unified communications and contact centre solution, which will be designed, implemented and supported by One Solution Mobile & Data.
Toyota Australia has deployed Promapp Solutions' process management software to aid in their transition to a national sales and distribution company from 2018.
The beta version of FuelCheck enables NSW consumers to get real-time information on fuel prices .
The Australian Communications Consumer Action Network (ACCAN) is lobbying for clearer information for consumers about broadband speed claims .
Sydney bus commuters will soon be able to access real-time info to find out whether they'll get a seat on the next bus . Travel app NextThere will be the first to go live with the new feature.
Trials of the new NAPLAN online testing platform have commenced at several Canberra schools.
HealthKit has partnered with CSIRO Data61 startup Coviu to integrate its browser-based telehealth platform to HealthKit's 15,000 health practitioners.
5 Catholic schools in remote Northern Territory communities are now using a web-based software system developed by Civica .
Flexiroam (ASX: FRX) has signed an agreement with TripAdvisor to leverage the TripAdvisor brand and content to increase Flexiroam X subscribers
Aeeris (ASX: AER) has secured two contracts to deploy its SecureAMS (Secure Access Management System) technology. The unnamed customers include an Australian shopping centre operator and a casino and hotel operator. The company also announced a marketing partnership agreement with Australian Security Technology.
London headquartered and NASDAQ listed data security company Mimecast has opened its new headquarters in Melbourne.
You will eventually be able to order pizza via drone delivery thanks to Domino's Pizza partnering with Flirtey. However, Australia will have to wait - New Zealand was selected as the launch market due to its current regulations around unmanned aircraft.
Tech23 industry leader Glenys Beauchamp (Department of Industry, Innovation and Science secretary) has spoken about how startups and early stage companies are most responsible for driving job creation. She was quoted in the Australian Financial Review last week.

people watch

Queensland's first Chief Entrepreneur is Blue Sky Alternative Investments founder Mark Sowerby.
John Goodall has been named Medibank 's (ASX: MPL) Executive General Manager Technology & Operations. Goodall was previously at GE Capital Australia and New Zealand.
Commercial drone operator Measure Australia has appointed Rebecca Fox as Business Development Manager for Residential Real Estate. The appointment is in response to increasing demand for drones as a service for businesses in real estate.
Omni-channel fulfilment platform Fluent Retail has named Graham Jackson as CEO. Jackson was previously Senior VP for APJ & China at SAP Hybris.
Chorus CEO Mark Ratcliffe is to step down from mid-2017.

company watch

New listings on the ASX this week include Frontier Digital Ventures Limited (ASX: FDV) and Family Zone Cyber Safety Limited (ASX: FZO).
Digital medical solutions provider DoseMe has closed a $2.6 million Series A financing round. Founders of US-based Results Physiotherapy Greg Spurgin and Gary Cunningham provided funding, and Spurgin will join seed investor Steve Baxter on the board.
Travello has closed its oversubscribed funding round, with 22 investors staking $1.26 million in the social network for travellers. Co-founder Mark Cantoni said that Travello attracted first-round equity investment from those who had not previously invested in technology companies.
