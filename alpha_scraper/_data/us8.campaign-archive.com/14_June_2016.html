news briefs

Sonder is collaborating with electronics contract manufacturer, Foxconn. Together they are developing a dynamic E ink Keyboard which adapts to display application-specific shortcuts, any language, or custom icons. Sonder has established an office at Foxconn’s Lafang campus in Beijing, China.
Tesco Mobile has partnered exclusively with Unlockd . Tesco Mobile Xtras helps customers reduce their monthly phone payments by £3 in exchange for viewing exclusive content and offers when unlocking their Android smartphones. Unlockd recently closed a $15 million series A Round with investors including SEEK Co-founder Matt Rockman and Virgin Australia and Citibank's board member Sam Mostyn.
The simple salt bath might change the future for rechargeable lithium batteries. Researchers from CSIRO, RMIT University and QUT have shown that pre-treating a battery's lithium metal electrodes with an electrolyte salt solution extends battery life and improves safety and performance. The research was published in Nature Communications . The technology could improve electric vehicle drive range and battery charge, making electric vehicles competitive with traditional petrol vehicles.
Finalists for the ACOMM Awards recognising excellence in the communications industry have been announced! The complete list of nominees across 11 categories is available here . Winners will be announced in Sydney on 20 July 2016 at the 10th annual ACOMMS Awards dinner
A bus has been transformed into a #ThinkDigitalCoach to provide digital education for regional, rural and remote Australia. The bus was launched at the Broadband for the Bush Forum last week.
Football (or is that soccer?) fans will be pleased as Optus expands its football offering through an agreement with Channel Nine, securing the broadcast and digital rights to 15 exclusive matches and three simulcast matches of the pre-season International Champions Cup tournament.
Sydney Water is on the hunt for a new technology partner. Expressions of Interest are sought for an implementation partner to design, build and run its enterprise resource planning, billing and customer relationship management solutions.
A partnership between Arc and UNSW Innovations has produced the FounderLab and Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Fund. The on-campus service provides professional product development and a funding format to address the challenges of reaching an MVP in order to attract investors.
ASIC has released a consultation paper , with proposals including a regulatory sandbox licensing exemption. The exemption would allow startups to test certain financial services for six months.
Queensland is investing $10 million into a Platform Technology Program to develop new platform technologies for business, in areas including drones, big data and IoT. The funding is part of a $405 million plan to accelerate the Advance Queensland Innovation initiative.
Ahead of a mass satellite launch from the International Space Station later this year, Australian researchers have begun testing satellites at the Mount Stromlo space testing facilities. Three 'CubeSats' - satellites built from cubes about 10 centimeters per side - have been developed by researchers from ANU , the University of Sydney, UNSW, University of Adelaide and University of South Australia. The CubeSats will be launched into space as part of the European Union's QB50 launch of 50 satellites.
Little-red flying foxes will sleep better with $2.7 million over three years in Queensland state funding to help monitor their movements and improve management of roosts in urban areas. CSIRO scientists will fit the animals with GPS transmitters to track their movements by satellite.

company watch

Seven West Media has secured a 15% shareholding in local services marketplace Airtasker and will provide media support and investment via Seven's national reach. Airtasker was founded in 2012 by Tim Fung (CEO) and Jonathan Lui (COO) who both previously worked as part of the founding team at MVNO Amaysim.
Ordermentum will be raising a glass after raising $2.5 million in a Series A capital round. Investors include Dean Gordon, Alison Deans, and David Skellern. The round was led by Capital Markets Technology.
New Zealand's telecommunications landscape is set to change with the SKY (ASX: SKT) and Vodafone NZ merger to create an 'integrated telecommunications and media group'. Vodafone will become a 51% shareholder in the combined group as a result of a consideration comprising an issue of new SKY shares and NZ$1250 million, totalling an enterprise value of $3,437 million.
9 Spokes (ASX: 9SP) has listed on the ASX. LiveHire Ltd (ASX: LVH) has also listed and raised $10 million .
Chapmans Opportunities Limited, a subsidiary of Chapmans Limited (ASX: CHP), is investing $5 million in Fantasy Sports Global Limited. The Hong Kong-based Ho family are also investing over $5 million.
OneVue (ASX: OVH) and Diversa Limited (ASX: DVA) are to merge . Under the agreement, OneVue will acquire all of Diversa's ordinary shares.
Tech Mpire Limited (ASX: TMP) has acquired Croatian software development company Appenture.

Drones 'as a service' appears to be on the rise, with private equity investor Matthew Hunter joining Measure Australia as Chairman. Hunter has also invested in the company through his investment firm, Rivendell Capital.
Vend 's former VP Strategy and CFO, Alex Fala, has been named as the company's new CEO.
Judy Wilson has moved from Samsung Electronics to take up the role of Channel Manager - Buying Groups at OKI .
MNF Group Limited (ASX: MNF) has appointed Lee Atkinson to Head of Products.
