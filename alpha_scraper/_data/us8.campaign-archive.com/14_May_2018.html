NEWS BRIEFS
Monash University will today launch and sign a research partnership with Honeywell Building Solutions . The collaboration will result in the construction of one of the world’s first ‘cognitive office’ buildings. Data collected from existing sites on campus will be used to reduce the building’s energy consumption.
Unlockd has been granted an injunction against Google. The Melbourne-headquartered Unlockd argued that the removal of its app from the Google Play Store was an unfair policy designed to limit competition.
LaunchVic is seeking to invest up to $1 million per application for Accelerator programs and up to $250,000 per application for founder and investor education services dedicated to the Health Startup sector.
Ringcentral has announced the launch of their first office in Australia as part of their Asia Pacific Expansion.
SA Water has deployed a network of over 400 sensors in water pipes running under the Adelaide CBD, allowing it to identify faults and leaks faster.
Startup Genome 's Melbourne Startup Ecosystem Report commissioned by LaunchVic has shown that Melbourne's startup sector has 1,100 tech startups and that 1 in 4 founders moved to Melbourne from overseas specifically to begin a startup.
Royal Perth Hospital and healthcare provider Emory Healthcare are jointly undertaking a remote monitoring program . Perth clinicians will use Philips’ eICU platform to remotely monitor critical care patients in Atlanta, Georgia during Australian daylight hours.
The latest recipients of the Victorian Government’s Horticulture Innovation Fund are Monash University, Apple and Pear Australia Ltd, Barengi Gadjin Land Council Aboriginal Corporation, the Australian Wine Research Institute, and Nursery and Garden Industry Victoria. The Fund aims to help the horticulture industry adapt to new production technologies through awarding grants of up to $50,000.
A new $5.9 million ACT government Centre for Innovation and Learning has opened at the Caroline Chisholm School in ACT.
The Federal Government has agreed to accept the recommendations put forward in February by the Review into Open Banking , with the implementation of the scheme to begin in July 2019.
Financial advisory startup myprosperity has announced a partnership with Macquarie Wealth .
AFL has announced a partnership with Riot Games that will see the organisations launch a new eSports tournament in November.
The Australian Prudential Regulation Authority has issued approval for fintech startup Volt to become a restricted authorised deposit-taking institution, making it Australia’s first ‘digital bank’.
Downer EDI Limited (ASX: DOW) has been awarded a $150 million contract by First Solar for construction of the 87MWac Beryl Solar Farm. The Farm will be located west of Gulgong, New South Wales.
Sydney-based wearable device vendor mCare Digital has partnered with data analytics firm WingArc Australia to bring data analysis functionality to mCare's ConnectiveCare platform.
BUDGET WATCH
The Federal Government has allocated $28 million to roll out a five year, nationwide electronic prescribing system . From October 2019, prescribing doctors can legally issue PBS scripts in electronic form.
Over $260 million has been committed to develop GPS and satellite technology . Of this amount, $36.9 million will be dedicated to Digital Earth Australia, a world-class technology that will give Australian businesses greater access to reliable, standardised satellite data.
The Digital Identity Govpass program will receive $92.4 million in funding, helping create a pilot Tax File Number application process. The Digital Transformation Agency has also been allocated $700,000 to support the development of blockchain technology in Government services.
A 12-year National Research Infrastructure Investment Plan will commit $1.9 billion to critical national research infrastructure, including $140 million for upgrades to the two most powerful computing facilities in the Southern Hemisphere.
$4.5 million has been allocated over four years as part of an initiative to encourage girls and women to pursue STEM education and career pathways.
Australia has announced plans to establish its first official national space agency after $41 million in funding was allocated to a four year funding scheme. The Government has also dedicated $15 million to international space investment.
COMPANY WATCH
NAB (ASX: NAB) has provided a $20 million debt facility to Mike Cannon-Brookes-backed home energy lender Brighte , which provides loans for households to set up solar panels and batteries. The last startup to receive funding from NAB was Afterpay.
Fintech startup Athena , which is disrupting home loans, has raised a $15 million Series A round, with funding coming from Macquarie Bank, Square Peg Capital, Apex Capital, and Rice Warner.
NPR, WNYC Studios, WBEZ Chicago and This American Life have acquired Pocket Casts and plan to run it as a joint venture. Pocket Casts is a podcast app created by Shifty Jelly, whose founders Philip Simpson and Russell Ivanovic will now hold leadership positions in the company.
Liesl Yearsley - who has been based in the US for some time - has received funding from Comet Labs for her new B-Corp AI venture Akin .

PEOPLE WATCH
Former CEO of Fishburners and Cofounder of Startup Muster Murray Hurps has been appointed as the new Director of Entrepreneurship at the University of Technology Sydney .
Victoria University has appointed Professor Marcia Devlin as their new Deputy Vice Chancellor. Prior to this appointment, Devlin was the Deputy Vice Chancellor (Academic) at RMIT University.
Commonwealth Bank (ASX: CBA) has appointed Alan Docherty as the Acting Chief Financial Officer following the resignation of Rob Jesudason, who has left the bank to take an executive role at the Hong Kong-based EOS cryptocurrency.
Megan Clarke has been appointed as the new head of the Australian Space Agency. Clarke will help launch the Australian Space Agency program over the next few years, with strong involvement from WA industry and science.
After six years at the helm, Steve Baxter is set to move on from Brisbane coworking hub River City Labs , announcing he is looking for a new owner.
