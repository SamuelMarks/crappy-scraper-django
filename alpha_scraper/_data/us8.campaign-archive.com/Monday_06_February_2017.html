NewsBriefs

The Jack Ma Foundation is establishing a $26.4 million scholarship program at the University of Newcastle . The founder and executive chairman of Alibaba, Jack Ma, has made the largest philanthropic commitment in UON's history as a tribute to his personal connection to Novocastrian Ken Morley. The Ma & Morley Scholarship Foundation aims to educate the next generation of Australia's leaders.
CSIRO has established an operational Urban Living Lab at the Sydney Science Park in western Sydney. Researchers will explore connections between urban issues (greening, energy efficiency, water demands and health) in relation to the impacts of technological advancements.
The ACCC has released its quarterly National Broadband Network Wholesale Market Indicators Report . Since the last reporting period, nbn co has increased its supply of broadband wholesale access service by 28% (an increase of over 316,000).
Tech23 and University of Melbourne MAP alumni, Black.ai is among the six startups chosen to be a part of Startmate 2017. Other companies include Construction Cloud ; Flaunter ; Mentorloop ; Morse Micro ; and Honee .
LaunchVic announced its seven startup Round 2 grant recipients , including: Girl Geek Academy , creators of Victoria’s first state-wide hackathon for girls; Seeva , developers of an innovation hub and incubator dedicated to the health and disability sector; and ACMI , who will extend its ACMI X into a co-working creative tech lab.
The ACCC has finalised the wholesale ADSL service declaration decision by continuing to regulate the service for another five years.
Drone technology is being used by the Northern Territory Government to survey and inspect the state’s legacy mine sites. Images and data from the drone can be used to mitigate risk and plan the next stages of work.
Curtin University's 2017 10-week Accelerate Program for selected students and alumni has commenced. Initiatives involved in this year’s program include a synthetic eardrum and wearable sensors for minesite health and safety.
The WA Government has given a funding boost to industry partner Women in Technology WA to encourage women to pursue an education and career in STEM .
e-Commerce giant Alibaba officially opened its Australia and New Zealand headquarters in Melbourne last week.
The Victorian Government has ' refreshed ' its contracts with telecommunications providers including Telstra, Optus, NEC, Vocus, MyNetFone, Mitel and R-Group, as part of its Telecommunications Purchasing and Management Strategy 2025.
Complexica Pty Ltd and Detmold Group are partnering to explore the use of AI and big data for sales and marketing functions.
iSignthis Ltd (ASX: ISX) will provide ayondo with its Paydentity service.
Members of the Regional, Rural and Remote Communications Coalition appeared at the Productivity Commission's enquiry into the Universal Service Obligation (USO) last week, to register concerns that rural phone services may be at risk under proposed changes to the USO.
CSIRO is making high-quality graphene (a carbon material the width of an atom, used in applications from miniaturised electronics to biomedical devices) from soybeans. The ' GraphAir ' technology reduces both cost of production and the need for a highly-controlled environment.
ANZ is partnering with Employment Hero to offer its customers free access to the cloud-based HR platform.
Tech23 Alumni​ Xped Limited (ASX: XPE) has signed a MoU with Solekai Systems to develop a binding agreement to license Xped technologies and market and develop solutions for Solekai's customer base.
Aged care provider Feros Care has opened a new technology hub . The Experience Centre will focus on the research, design and implementation of digital technologies for seniors and people with disability. The Centre includes a research lab, 'Plug and Play Centre', knowledge centre, virtual care team and a warehouse and distribution centre.
The University of Wollongong , in collaboration with iAccelerate , has announced a $10 million raising to support entrepreneurs and startups to help develop their ideas.
The State Library of Victoria is deploying Ex Libris ' Rosetta program to manage its digital assets and resources .
Telstra Health has been awarded a $10 million contract for a Community Health Information System in WA. The system will provide an electronic clinical record for patients using WA Country Health Service's community and primary health centres. The system will first be introduced in the Kimberley in May of this year.
A collaboration between the NSW Government, universities and industry will investigate how small, smart devices can facilitate on-site measurements and remote tracking of health and the environment. The NSW Smart Sensing Network was launched last week at the Australian Institute for Nanoscale Science and Technology headquarters.

PeopleWatch

Natalie Dinsdale is Tyro Payments Limited 's new Head of Brand and Marketing.
Citrix has appointed Les Williamson as Area Vice President of Australia and New Zealand. Williamson was previously with Cisco.
Sarah Pearson , CEO and Founder of CBR Innovation Network, is taking up the role of Pro Vice-Chancellor for Industry Engagement and Innovation at the University of Newcastle . Pearson commences in mid-March.
Former Woolworths senior executive Emma Gray has been appointed as the ANZ Bank’s first Chief Data Officer.
Melbourne-headquartered mobile ad platform Unlockd has hired former Microsoft global head of innovation Jonathan Oliver .
Get Swift has appointed former Qantas CIO Jamila Gordon as its global Chief Information Officer and Board Member.
Jetstar 's new Head of Cyber Security is Yvette Lejins . Lejins was previously with Asciano and, prior to that, Atlassian .

CompanyWatch

Inabox (ASX: IAB) will acquire Hostworks for $7 million , including a $5 million upfront payment - with the rest deferred to FY18. Hostworks was founded by Marty Gauvin in 1999 and then sold to Broadcast Australia (a BAI Communications company) in 2007 for close to $70 million.
MNF Group (ASX: MNF) will acquire Conference Call International for $18 million, funded through a heavily oversubscribed placement.
WiseTech Global (ASX:WTC) has acquired German customs solutions provider znet group .
