news briefs

Postal service start-up Sendle has partnered with Toll to provide delivery services for small businesses normally reserved for big businesses and enterprise.
Google Australia has announced $1 million in funding will be given to the Australian Indigenous Mentoring Experience , FIRST Robotics Australia , and Engineers Without Borders Australia to encourage STEM education in Australia by delivering hands-on training and career programs aimed at reaching girls, indigenous Australians, and students from low socio-economic and regional areas.
A new online account function will be available through Service NSW for NSW citizens to manage their details online and via a mobile app. The online account functionality will be available from September 2015.
The Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC) has released its first ever unclassified cyber security threat report. New ACSC Co-ordinator, Clive Lines said that the report clearly demonstrates that the cyber threat to Australian organisations is undeniable, unrelenting and continues to grow. The report also contains mitigation and remediation information to assist organisations to prevent and respond to the threat.
The Board of Studies, Teaching and Educational Standards have selected UK software company Civica ’s MarkManager to manage the online marking of the Higher School Certificate examinations.
The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) has selected the Australian National University (ANU) to host its W3C Australia Office. The Office will be jointly hosted by the ANU College of Business and Economics and the ANU College of Engineering and Computer Science.
The Australian Industry Group , Australian Information Industry Association , Australian Mobile Telecommunications Association and Communications Alliance have made a joint Submission to the Attorney-General’s Department on the Exposure Draft Telecommunications and Other Legislation Amendment Bill 2015. The associations are unable to support the proposed Bill as described in the exposure draft legislation, for reasons including that it constitutes regulatory ‘over-reach'.
The Queensland University of Technology has used Google Cardboard to create a virtual reality experience for prospective students. The Global Goggles campaign created by BCM, shows a 360-degree rendering of the Brisbane skyline as well as other international landmarks.
The National Australia Bank (NAB) has announced it will establish a $50 million Venture fund, deployed over three years here and overseas. NAB Ventures will be part of NAB Labs, a dedicated innovation capability designed to build a culture of innovation and customer-led design within NAB.
The nbn has announced it will commit nearly $40 million for training and awareness campaigns to attract an extra 4,500 workers into the industry to support the accelerated connection of eight million homes to the nbn network by 2020.
UXC Limited (ASX: UXC) has signed a $25 million contract with Ausgrid and Endeavour Energy to provide service desk and end user computing support services for 7,500 users across NSW over the next three years, with the option of two two-year extensions.
The Australian Communications and Media Authority has signed a multi-year contract with US software company Power Auctions for the delivery of software capability designed to conduct spectrum auctions.
River City Labs , the co-working startup space set up by Pipe Networks Co-founder Steve Baxter has partnered with Muru-D to develop the River City Labs Accelerator program. Applications are now open, with the 14 week programme starting in September.
The Australian Securities and Investment Commission has updated its regulatory guidelines to make it more technology-neutral.

company watch

SpeedCast (ASX: SDA) has signed a definitive agreement to acquire satellite communications provider SAIT Communications for an initial consideration of US $14.2 million on a cash free/debt free basis.
Brisbane based employee training startup GO1 has closed $1 million investment round led by entrepreneur and investor Steve Baxter and Tank Stream Ventures .
Neustar, Inc . (NYSE:NSR) has acquired Bombora Technologies Pty Ltd (formerly AusRegistry Group) for $118.5 million.

people watch

JB Hi-Fi has appointed Australia Post ’s GM Information, Digital and Technology Simon Page to join the company as its first CIO. Page has previously held roles with Accenture and Tabcorp Australia .
