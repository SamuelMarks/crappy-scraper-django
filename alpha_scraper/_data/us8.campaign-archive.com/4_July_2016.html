news briefs

ABC News and Current Affairs received 2.8 million visitors, 4.3 million visits and 20.5 million page views - doubling the traffic recorded for the 2013 election. Smartphone visits drove the increase in traffic, accounting for 57% of all visits (compared to 33% for the previous election).
The University of Melbourne has launched Spartan , a new high-performance computing (HPC) service with a flexible cloud computing component. Spartan uses the open-source Linux operating system and SLURM as a workload manager. Physical hardware includes Intel systems sourced from Dell and switches from Mellanox and Cisco.
Australian researchers have successfully completed 'Astronomical Verification' of a sub-system of the Square Kilometre Array (SKA).
If you're a fan of the Tour de France or are counting down to the Rio Olympics, you can now access customised 3D interactive map visualisations of routes through doarama.com, a startup out of CSIRO's Data61 group. Doarama was originally launched in 2013 and has since been revamped.
Aconex (ASX: ACX) has signed a four-year enterprise agreement with ExxonMobil Global Services Company.
Young companies with clever tech and the potential for high-growth are invited to apply to be part of the eighth annual Tech23 event on Tuesday 11 October 2016 in Sydney. Applications close 29 August 2016.
Mining company Minara Resources has adopted new CSIRO drag reduction technology . The technology saves energy and water use.
You can read the consultation report and final plan for City of Sydney 's tech startup action plan .
The City of Sydney plans to source 50% of its electricity from renewable energy by 2030 and reach net zero emissions by 2050.
New tax rules for investments have begun with the new financial year! Angel investors are now eligible to claim a 20% tax offset and capital gains tax exemption for investments up to $1M in an eligible early-stage innovation company. Larger institutional investors are eligible to claim a 10% tax offset for investments into a $200 million early-stage venture capital limited partnership.
Visage Imaging - the wholly owned US subsidiary of health imaging company Pro Medicus Limited (ASX: PME) - has signed an $18 million, six-year contract with Mayo Clinic. The contract will see Pro Medicus' Visage 7 technology implemented across Mayo Clinic's Radiology practices.
Australian startup Yondo has partnered with Weebly as the sole providers of webinar services to Weeblys 30 million global customers via the Weebly App Center.
Impurities are improving the performance of tiny lasers. ANU scientists have added atoms of zinc to lasers which improves by 100 the amount of light from the lasers. The move will aid in the development of low cost biomedical sensors, quantum computing and fast internet.

company watch

Vocus will acquire Nextgen Networks and two development projects - North West Cable System and Australia Singapore Cable - for $807 million . Vocus also completed a capital raising of approximately $652 million to support its acquisition.
Readify - a Blue Sky Private Equity portfolio company - has been acquired by Telstra for an undisclosed sum. Readify's management team will remain in place. Readify employs around 200 staff, including 160 software developers.
Employment Hero plans to expand into the UK with the $3 million theyve raised in a funding round led by OneVentures, AMP New Ventures, and an anonymous high-net-worth investor. This is the the first capital raise for the previously boot-strapped HR tech company.
After raising $4.3 million as part of a reverse takeover of Credo Resources Limited (ASX: CRQ), Vault Intelligence Limited (ASX: VLT) has commenced trading on the ASX.
Rision (ASX: RNL) has acquired cloud-based rostering software provider RosterCloud which was founded by Rohan Workman who heads up the University of Melbournes Accelerator Program.
